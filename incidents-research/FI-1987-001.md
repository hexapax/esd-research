# FI-1987-001 — Research Notes

## Incident Summary (from source PDF)
An 18-year-old boy fell off a dock at Bay Haven Marina on Lake Macatawa, Park Township, Michigan, and drowned. The source document notes that two rescue attempts were thwarted because rescuers experienced severe electric shock upon entering the water. The year is listed as "1987 or 1988" in the source document, with the file assigned to 1987. Lake Macatawa is a freshwater lake connected to Lake Michigan near Holland, Michigan.
