# FI-1998-001 — Research Notes

## Incident Summary (from source PDF)

Approximately September 1998, Lake Sonoma, California. A young girl drowned in fresh water. She was described as being "in great distress." A fault was found on the dock. Listed as entry #56 in the Shafer/Rifkin ESD incident list (PDF item #100). Minimal details recorded; approximate date only.
