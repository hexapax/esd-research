# FI-1998-002 — Research Notes

## Incident Summary (from source PDF)

In September 1998 in Rio Vista, California, two boys aged 8–10 drowned at a marina. Prior to the fatal drowning, other boys had reported feeling a tingle in the water and exited; those warning signs were not acted upon. The cause was determined to be 40-year-old faulty wiring running under moored boats that had failed insulation and substantial fault to ground.
