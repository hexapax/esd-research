# Research: FI-2019-001 — Lake Pleasant Brothers Electric Shock Drowning (2020)

## Original Incident Summary

Per the ESDPA-derived file, on Sep 19, 2019, at Scorpion Bay Marina on Lake Pleasant near Peoria, Arizona, two brothers were killed in the water after the first started screaming when he jumped in next to their boat. Two others jumped in and were rescued and treated. Investigators found a modification to the shore power cable system which energized the boat's underwater metals. It took 10 minutes to get the power secured for rescue operations.

Multiple independent sources (12 News, ABC15, Fox News, EC&M, Peoria Times) all confirm the incident occurred on **July 12, 2020**. Both the month (September vs. July) and year (2019 vs. 2020) are incorrect. The date should be corrected to "July 12, 2020."

2. **The original file mentions the date discrepancy between ESD section (Sep 19, 2019) and NM section (Aug 12, 2020).** Neither date is correct. The actual date was July 12, 2020, which is different from both dates listed in the ESDPA document.

3. **Description is generally accurate but incomplete.** The original correctly states "2 brothers were killed" and mentions "modification to the shore power cable system which energized boat's underwater metals." However, more precisely:
   - The victims were Timothy Miller (53) and Michael Miller (50)
   - The boat had an electrical connection system incompatible with the marina's receptacle

4. **"Two others jumped in and were rescued and treated" is accurate.** Michael's wife jumped in and suffered electrical shock with burn marks on her feet and legs but survived. Another woman (described as either Timothy's wife or girlfriend in different sources) also jumped in and was rescued.

5. **"Took 10 min to get the power secured for rescue operations" is plausible but not specifically confirmed in sources.** The sources confirm that power had to be secured before recovery operations, but the specific "10 minutes" timeframe was not mentioned in any of the independent sources reviewed.
