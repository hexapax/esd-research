## Corrections Needed

1. **DATE IS WRONG.** The original file states "June 24, 2006." All independent sources — the Times Herald headline ("7-14-2006"), the obituary ("died unexpectedly as a result of an accident on Friday, July 14, 2006"), and Find a Grave — consistently confirm the date was **July 14, 2006**. July 14, 2006 was a Friday, which cross-checks with the obituary text. The date should be corrected from June 24 to July 14, 2006.

2. **"Disabled" descriptor needs clarification.** The raw file says the victim was "disabled climbing onto swim platform." Independent sources describe him as "attempting to board a docked boat" when he was incapacitated by the electrical shock. The word "disabled" in the raw file means he was **electrically disabled/incapacitated** while climbing — not that he had a pre-existing physical disability. Michael McNash was a lifeguard, multi-sport athlete, and active young man.

3. **Two friends shocked — partially confirmed.** The raw file states "two friends shocked; could not be resuscitated." The Times Herald describes the incident mechanism (faulty shore power cord) but the available extract does not explicitly describe the friends being shocked. The raw file's "Shocked/survived: 2" is plausible given the ESD scenario but is not independently confirmed in the available source text.
