Dr. Hughes had been cutting grass, went swimming to cool off, and was found in approximately 4 feet of water near his boat.

However, the exact source of the electrical current (boat, dock, or other equipment) is not specified in available sources.

6. **Day of week verification.** August 8, 2015, was a Saturday, which is consistent with Dr. Hughes being at his lake property doing yard work (cutting grass) and going for a recreational swim.

7. **Context about Lake Tuscaloosa ESD history.** It's worth noting that Lake Tuscaloosa has had multiple electric shock drowning incidents. Dr. Hughes' death in 2015 was followed by the deaths of two women in 2017 in similar circumstances, suggesting ongoing electrical safety issues at the lake.
