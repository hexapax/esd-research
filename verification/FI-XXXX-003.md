**Technical cause identified in investigation:**
- The power cord from an adjacent soft drink machine had been pinched under the candy machine's metal leg
- The outer insulation gradually became damaged, exposing the energized conductor
- The energized conductor made contact with the metal leg of the candy machine
- **Critically, the grounding pin on the candy machine's power cord was broken**
- This combination caused the candy machine's metal cabinet to become energized
- Investigators recreated the conditions and found "almost full line voltage between the candy machine cabinet and the wet tiled floor"

**Post-incident actions:**

- The recreational center installed rubber mats in front of vending machines
- The center instituted a safety inspection policy for vending machines
