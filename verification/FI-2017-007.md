# Research: FI-2017-007 — Kayla Matos, 11, electrocuted by boat lift, Toms River NJ

## Original Incident Summary

**Date:** Jun 17, 2017
**Location:** Toms River Lagoon, New Jersey
**Victim:** 11-year-old girl
**Outcome:** Fatal; 2 other girls uninjured

**Summary from ESDPA list:** An 11 year old girl was killed when she touched a boat lift that was electrified. She and 2 friends were swimming and on a raft near the lift. The 11 year old reached up to touch the boat lift and was electrocuted. The boat lift had not been used in years. The electrical junction box was corroded from the weather. The girls were wearing life jackets. The other 2 girls were not injured.
