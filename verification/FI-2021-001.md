## Source Conflicts and Discrepancies

1. **Cause of death disputed by yacht club:** The Oldham County Coroner confirmed electrocution as the cause of death. However, the yacht club's attorney Berry Baxter disputed the coroner's findings, arguing that other divers entered the water to recover Elkins' body and were not electrocuted (WAVE3, August 16, 2022).

2. **OSHA jurisdictional gap:** OSHA declined to investigate because Elkins was classified as an independent contractor with no employer-to-employee relationship. Both the Kentucky Labor Cabinet and U.S. Coast Guard referred the case to OSHA, creating a jurisdictional gap where no federal agency investigated.

3. **Stop work order timeline:** A state building inspector issued a stop work order on April 20, 2022 (two days before Elkins' death) for lack of building permits. A prior stop work order had been issued in November 2021. Despite these orders, work apparently continued.

4. **Electrical permit gap:** No electrical permits had been issued by Oldham County for the yacht club's gas pumps in 2021 or 2022, meaning gas pump electrical systems were uninspected.
