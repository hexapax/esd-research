# FI-2025-001 — Research File

## Original Incident Summary

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | July 11, 2025 |
| **Location** | Porter County, Indiana — Marina Shores marina |
| **Classification** | Fatal Incident |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 (21yo man), Shocked/survived: 1 (23yo brother pulled to safety) |

**Description:** Porter County Indiana, Marina Shores marina. The investigation revealed an object that belonged to a boat owner fell into the water from the dock and began floating away. A 21-year-old voluntarily went into the water to retrieve the object for its owner. As he swam back to dock he appeared to be in distress. His 23-year-old brother jumped in to rescue but was also overcome by electricity in the water. He was pulled to safety. The 21-year-old was later recovered from the water. Investigators and a licensed electrician determined the likely source of the electricity was from equipment belonging to a nearby slip holder.
