# Research: FI-1999-005

## Original Incident Summary
In approximately July 1999, a woman was in great distress in 3 feet of freshwater in South Carolina. Her husband attempted to rescue her and drowned. The incident is classified as a fatal electric shock drowning (ESD) with 2 dead, listed as item #98 in the Shafer & Rifkin ESD incident compilation (Rev. 8/15/2025). The original file notes a contradiction: the source describes a "single drowning" but the narrative indicates the husband also drowned during the rescue, making it likely a double fatality.
