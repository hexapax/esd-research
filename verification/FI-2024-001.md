**Description:** Smith Mountain Lake, Huddleston, VA. An 18yo man jumped into the water at the lake. After recognizing the man was in distress, 2 others jumped in to help. They felt the electrical shock and were able to get out of the water. The 18yo perished from the electric shock drowning. Details pending.
