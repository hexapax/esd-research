# Research: FI-2017-012 — Lake Tuscaloosa, Alabama Electric Shock Drowning (2017)

## Original Incident Summary

Per the ESDPA-derived file, on March 29, 2017, at Lake Tuscaloosa, Alabama, two women went missing after sunbathing on a dock at the lake. Their bodies were found the next morning. At least one investigator felt an electrical shock on the dock during initial emergency response. The accident has been ruled an Electric Shock Drowning.
