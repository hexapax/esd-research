## Corrections Needed

1. **DATE IS WRONG.** The original file states "July 12, 2020." Multiple independent sources (ABC13, Click2Houston, Texas Wrongful Death Lawyer) confirm the incident occurred on **August 29, 2020**. One source (Click2Houston on Sept 2) states August 31, 2020, but the preponderance of evidence points to August 29, 2020. The date should be corrected from "July 12, 2020" to "August 29, 2020" (a difference of nearly 7 weeks).

2. **Victim identification.** The original file only states "teenager." The victim was **Khaleel Marcos Reynolds, age 15**. Adding the victim's name would improve the record.

3. **Location could be more specific.** The original says "Harris County, Texas — hotel pool." The specific location was the **North Villa Inn** (also called North Villa Hotel and Banquet Hall), located at 16510 North Freeway in north Harris County, Houston.

4. **Mechanism is accurate but could be more detailed.** The original correctly states "Light fixture wires were exposed and the teen allegedly came in contact with them." Independent sources confirm that someone had removed the interior light from its housing, leaving wires exposed. The word "allegedly" is appropriate given the investigative context, though news reports consistently confirm this was the cause.

5. **Pool inspection history is confirmed.** The original correctly states "The pool failed several inspections prior in an attempt to open." This is confirmed and can be made more specific: the pool failed inspections in July 2018, December 2018, and again on August 30, 2020 (the day after the fatality). The hotel never obtained a permit to operate the pool.

6. **Classification notes are accurate.** The original correctly identifies this as a direct contact electrocution rather than pure ESD, though it's included in the ESD Drownings list due to the water environment and similar hazard profile.
