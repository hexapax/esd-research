## Corrections Needed

1. **DATE IS WRONG.** The original file states "June 30, 2013." Multiple independent sources (CBS Minnesota citing the Sherburne County Sheriff's Office, WaveLink Secure blog) report the incident occurred on **July 4, 2013** (Independence Day/Fourth of July). The date should be corrected from "June 30, 2013" to "July 4, 2013." The death date of July 7, 2013, is correct.

2. **Victim identification.** The original does not name the victim. Independent sources identify him as **Daniel Petersen, age 46, of Big Lake, Minnesota.**

3. **Additional victim not mentioned.** The original file does not mention that a second person was injured: **Petersen's 49-year-old sister was also shocked but survived.** She ran to his aid when he was electrocuted and was shocked herself. Neighbors provided medical assistance and she was breathing on her own at the scene. The "Outcome" field should be updated to reflect "Dead: 1, Injured: 1" or "Dead: 1, Shocked/survived: 1."

4. **More complete description of the incident.** The original states "Man immersed plugged-in battery charger while standing in water." While accurate, additional details clarify the sequence of events:
   - Petersen was standing in approximately 20 inches of water
   - He was preparing to connect the charger to his **houseboat** (not just a regular boat)
   - He **slipped** and fell, causing him to immerse the plugged-in charger
   - The immersion resulted in severe electrical shock that instantly paralyzed his muscles
   - His sister ran to his aid and was also shocked when she tried to help
   - Neighbors intervened by unplugging the electrical cord and pulling both victims from the water
