# ESD-2019-06-02-1: Universal Volcano Bay Water Park — Orlando, FL

## Summary

On June 2, 2019, numerous staff and guests were shocked at Universal's Volcano Bay water park. Five lifeguards were hospitalized (released same day). OSHA found the cause was a construction defect — a grounding rod driven through electrical conduit. No fines were issued. At least one guest lawsuit was filed.
