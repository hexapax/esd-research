# ESD-2014-04-27-1: Palms West Condominiums Pool — Hialeah, FL

## Summary

On April 27, 2014, three children (Diego Cabrera, 10; Daniella Gamez, 6; and an unnamed 5-year-old girl) were shocked by an improperly grounded pool pump at the Palms West Condominiums pool in Hialeah, Florida. The 5-year-old was paralyzed by electricity after touching the metal hand rail. An adult rescuer was also shocked during the rescue. All children were hospitalized for four nights and recovered. Surveillance video documented the incident.
