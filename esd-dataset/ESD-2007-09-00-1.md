# ESD-2007-09-00-1: Owner Shocked Cleaning Prop Shaft, Sees Blue Sparks — Caloosahatchee River FL

## Summary

In September 2007, a boat owner cleaning a propeller shaft in the water at Franklin Lock Campground on the Caloosahatchee River, Florida experienced severe electric shock. His teeth clenched involuntarily from the current, and he observed blue sparks from arcing. He managed to exit the water. The circuit breaker had tripped.
