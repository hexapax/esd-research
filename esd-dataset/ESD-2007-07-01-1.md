# ESD-2007-07-01-1: SCUBA Diver Shocked Near Sailboat — Collins Bay, Kingston Ontario Canada

## Summary

On July 1, 2007, a SCUBA diver felt tingling then moderate shock while approaching a moored sailboat at Collins Bay on Lake Ontario, Kingston, Ontario, Canada. The diver backed away safely. A battery charger on the sailboat with a short circuit was identified as the cause.
