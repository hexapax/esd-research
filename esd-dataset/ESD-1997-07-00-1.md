# ESD-1997-07-00-1: Unknown Boy — Air Force Base, Washington DC

## Summary

A boy walking on ice at a dock on an Air Force base in Washington, DC, slipped and grabbed exposed electrical wires and was electrocuted. The ESDPA list dates the incident to July 1997, but this conflicts with the description of ice conditions, which are impossible in Washington, DC in summer. The actual date was likely a winter month. This is the second fatal electrocution of a boy at the same facility. No independent sources have been found.
