# ESD-2015-00-00-1: Dianne Guyton (Survivor) — Smith Lake, AL

## Summary

Approximately 2015, Dianne Guyton was electrocuted while swimming in Smith Lake, Alabama, when she reached up to a dock and encountered electricity in the water. Neighbors rescued her. She survived and later became an ESD awareness advocate. This occurred at the same lake where Carmen Johnson was killed by ESD in April 2016.
