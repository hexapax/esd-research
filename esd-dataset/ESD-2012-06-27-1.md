# ESD-2012-06-27-1: Ashton Jojo — Orange Lake Resort, FL

## Summary

On June 27, 2012, Ashton Jojo, 11, from Latham, New York, was electrocuted at the miniature golf course at Orange Lake Resort near Kissimmee, Florida. She stepped into a 2-foot-deep pond to retrieve her golf ball and was shocked by a faulty pump connected to the wrong circuit breaker. A bystander who attempted rescue was also injured. The family's $27M lawsuit settled for $10M.
