# ESD-2016-04-16-1: Carmen Johnson — Smith Lake, AL

## Summary

On April 16, 2016, Carmen Elizabeth Johnson, 15, was electrocuted at her family's lakehouse dock on Smith Lake, Alabama. A water-filled electrical box and non-functioning GFCI allowed current to energize the dock. Her father and brother were also shocked during rescue. She died the following day. The Johnson family became national ESD awareness advocates.
