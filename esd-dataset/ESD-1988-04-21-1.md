# ESD-1988-04-21-1: Bryan Higgins — SUNY Albany, NY

## Summary

On the evening of April 21, 1988, Bryan Higgins, a 20-year-old sophomore from Mahopac, New York, was electrocuted while wading into Indian Lake, a campus pond at SUNY Albany, during a Tau Kappa Epsilon fraternity initiation ritual. A faulty underground electrical cable near a pump house energized the pond. Two other students who entered the water to help were shocked but survived. Higgins was pronounced dead at Albany Medical Center; the autopsy confirmed cardiac arrest due to electrocution.
