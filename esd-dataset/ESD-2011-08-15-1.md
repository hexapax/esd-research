# ESD-2011-08-15-1: Unknown Woman (25) — Lake Sinclair, Putnam County GA

## Summary

On August 15, 2011, a 25-year-old woman was electrocuted at Lake Sinclair in Putnam County, Georgia. She was in the water and reached up to touch either a box or bare wire between two docked houseboats. The dock was being worked on at the time. An 8-year-old girl also swimming in the area received a shock but survived. A second woman was shocked when she attempted to pull the victim away from the dock and hold her head above water.
