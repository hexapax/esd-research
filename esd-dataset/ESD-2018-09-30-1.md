# ESD-2018-09-30-1: Wesley Seeley — Bricktown Canal, Oklahoma City, OK

## Summary

On September 30, 2018, Wesley Seeley, 23, was electrocuted in Oklahoma City's Bricktown Canal after a loose bollard light gave way, sending him into the water. The ungrounded bollards had been scheduled for removal months earlier. An $8M jury verdict was awarded against the electrical contractor.
