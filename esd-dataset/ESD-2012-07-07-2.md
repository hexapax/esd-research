# ESD-2012-07-07-2: Jennifer Lankford — Lake of the Ozarks, Dry Branch Cove MO

## Summary

On Saturday, July 7, 2012, Jennifer Lankford, 26, of Hazelwood, Missouri, was electrocuted at Dry Branch Cove on Lake of the Ozarks, Morgan County, at the 5-mile marker. Lankford was swimming with two stepbrothers (ages 11 and 13) when she touched a dock with faulty wiring at approximately 7:45 p.m. The children survived. This was the second fatal dock electrocution at Lake of the Ozarks that week, occurring just three days after siblings Alexandra and Brayden Anderson were killed at a different location on the same lake on July 4. The cluster of three ESD deaths in one week prompted significant calls for dock electrical safety reform.
