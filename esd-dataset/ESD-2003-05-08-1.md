# ESD-2003-05-08-1: Alex Kopinetz and D.J. Brandenburg — North Fort Myers, FL

## Summary

On May 8, 2003, two teenage boys were electrocuted in a canal in North Fort Myers, Lee County, Florida. Douglas "Bam-Bam" Brandenburg Jr., 15, jumped into the canal to retrieve a football and was electrocuted upon grabbing an electrified boat lift attached to a dock. Alex "Squirly" Kopinetz, 16, called for nearby adults and jumped into the water to rescue his friend but was also electrocuted when he touched the boat lift. Resuscitation attempts by eyewitnesses and paramedics failed, and both boys were pronounced dead at Cape Coral Hospital. Investigation by Lee County Sheriff's Office found that rebar had penetrated through a power cable to the boat lift, energizing the lift frame.
