# ESD-2000-09-30-2: Unknown Young Man — Put-in-Bay, OH

## Summary

On September 30, 2000, a young bystander was electrocuted after entering the water to retrieve sunglasses near a Grand Banks 42 powerboat at Put-in-Bay, Ohio, on Lake Erie. No victim name is known. This incident could not be independently verified, and news coverage of a 2017 ESD death at Put-in-Bay described it as the first such incident ever reported in Ohio.
