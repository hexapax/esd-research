# ESD-2012-07-04-2: Noah Winstead & Nate Lynam — Cherokee Lake, TN

## Summary

On July 4, 2012, Noah Dean Winstead (10) and Nate Lynam (11) were electrocuted while swimming at German Creek Marina on Cherokee Lake in Bean Station, Tennessee. The boys were playing near a metal ladder on a houseboat when frayed wiring on the vessel energized the water. Noah died that afternoon; Nate died the following day on life support. Nate's 8-year-old sister was also shocked but survived. Their deaths led to Tennessee's "Noah Dean and Nate Act" (2014), requiring GFCI installation at marinas statewide.
