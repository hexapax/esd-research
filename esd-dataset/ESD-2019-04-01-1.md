# ESD-2019-04-01-1: Jacob Hourmouzus & Jacob Schneider — Dixon, CA

## Summary

On April 1, 2019, two Dixon High School students — Jacob Hourmouzus and Jacob Schneider, both 17 — were electrocuted after jumping into an irrigation canal to rescue a dog and grabbing an energized metal bridge. A bypassed circuit breaker was alleged as the cause. The Solano Irrigation District settled for $14 million.
