# ESD-1997-07-00-2: Unknown Victim — Lake Mead, NV

## Summary

In July 1997, an unknown person drowned at Lake Mead, Nevada, attributed to a houseboat electrical fault. This is the only documented ESD incident at Lake Mead despite it being one of America's busiest recreational lakes. No independent sources have been found.
