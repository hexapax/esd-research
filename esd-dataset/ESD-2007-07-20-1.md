# ESD-2007-07-20-1: Man Paralyzed at Dock, Wife Holds Head Above Water — Lake Arcadia, Edmond OK

## Summary

On July 20, 2007, an adult male was immediately paralyzed by electric shock at the end of a dock at Lake Arcadia, Edmond, Oklahoma. His wife kept his head above water from the dock, preventing drowning. A bystander was also shocked. Investigation found that high water levels had submerged electrical outlets at the dock.
