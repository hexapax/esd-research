# ESD-2010-09-05-1: 12-Year-Old Girl Suffers Cardiac Arrest at Dock — Hartwell Lake, Anderson SC

## Summary

On September 5, 2010, a 12-year-old girl grabbed an electrical wire at Hartwell Lake, Anderson, South Carolina and went into cardiac arrest. Her father performed CPR and resuscitated her. She was airlifted to the hospital. A grounding problem on the dock was identified as the cause. She survived.
