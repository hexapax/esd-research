# ESD-1993-09-05-1: Unknown Male (46) — Northeastern Oklahoma Lake

## Summary

On September 5, 1993, a 46-year-old male scuba diver was electrocuted after contacting an energized submersible water pump at a lake in northeastern Oklahoma. A witness observed a blue softball-size ball of flame emitting from a pipe near the pump. The medical examiner ruled the cause of death as electrocution. This was one of five electricity-related deaths at the same Oklahoma lake system between 1989 and 1993, as documented by the CDC.
