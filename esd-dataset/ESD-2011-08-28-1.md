# ESD-2011-08-28-1: Dog Electrocuted, Women Shocked in Hurricane Irene Floodwater — Norwalk CT

## Summary

On August 28, 2011, during Hurricane Irene, a dog was electrocuted in floodwater near Shady Beach, Norwalk, Connecticut. Two women felt electric shocks in the water. Witnesses reported the "water was boiling" from the electrical current. The source was likely downed power lines or submerged electrical equipment from the storm.
