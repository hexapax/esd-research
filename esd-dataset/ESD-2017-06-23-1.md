# ESD-2017-06-23-1: Water Park Mass Casualty — Akyazi, Turkey

## Summary

On June 23, 2017, five people died at a water park in Akyazi, Turkey, after the pool became electrified due to a missing residual current breaker. Three children were caught in the current; the park manager and his son died attempting rescue. Two others were injured from touching energized metal railings.
