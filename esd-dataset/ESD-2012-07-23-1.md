# ESD-2012-07-23-1: Children and Adult Shocked at Dock Under Repair — Lake of the Ozarks MO

## Summary

On July 23, 2012, two children and an adult near a dock undergoing electrical repairs at the Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri all felt electric shock. Power was turned off and everyone exited safely. This incident occurred during the heightened safety crisis at Lake of the Ozarks following two fatal ESD incidents in early July 2012.
