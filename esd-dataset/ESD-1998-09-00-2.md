# ESD-1998-09-00-2: Two Boys (ages 8-10) — Rio Vista, CA

## Summary

In approximately September 1998, two boys ages 8-10 drowned at a marina in Rio Vista, California, on the Sacramento River Delta. Before the fatal incident, other boys had reported feeling a tingle while in the water and had gotten out. The cause was approximately 40-year-old faulty wiring running under moored boats with insulation failure leading to ground faults. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
