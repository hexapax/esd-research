# ESD-2010-07-25-1: Zachary Crays — Lake Freeman, IN

## Summary

On July 25, 2010, Zachary Crays, 13, was electrocuted while swimming near a dock at Angel Shores Resort on Lake Freeman, Indiana. A water-soaked junction box energized the dock. A $3M wrongful death judgment was awarded, with fault assigned 95% to the property lessee and 5% to the property owner.
