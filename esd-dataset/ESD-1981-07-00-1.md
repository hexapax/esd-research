# ESD-1981-07-00-1: Diver Feels Electric Pulses While Checking Zincs — Connecticut River, Essex CT

## Summary

In July 1981, a diver checking zincs in the Connecticut River at Essex, Connecticut felt strong electric pulses in the brackish water. The diver backed away safely. The pulses ceased after boat power was secured. No injuries resulted.
