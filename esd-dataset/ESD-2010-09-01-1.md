# ESD-2010-09-01-1: Dog Electrocuted in Park Lake, Man Stopped by Shocks — Palmetto Bay FL

## Summary

On September 1, 2010, a 6-month-old Labrador retriever named Ruger died from electrocution in the small lake at Perrine Wayside Dog Park in Palmetto Bay, Florida. A man (the dog's owner, Victor Garcia) was stopped by shocks when attempting to enter the water to rescue the dog. The fountain continued electrifying the water even after being turned off. The fountain, pump, and wiring were completely removed and the park was closed for most of September.
