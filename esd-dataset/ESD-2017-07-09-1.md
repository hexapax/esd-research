# ESD-2017-07-09-1: Madison Coe — Lovington, NM

## Summary

On July 9, 2017, Madison Coe, 14, was electrocuted in a bathtub at her father's home in Lovington, NM. She contacted a frayed extension cord while using her phone. The U.S. Consumer Product Safety Commission investigated. Her family released her final text message to raise awareness.
