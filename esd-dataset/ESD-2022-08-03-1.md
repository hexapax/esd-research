# ESD-2022-08-03-1: Marina Employee — Mobile Boat Lift Overhead Line Contact

## Summary

On August 3, 2022, a marina employee was electrocuted when the mobile boat lift he was operating to move boats into slips contacted overhead power lines. He fell from the operator's cab and died at the scene. This is not ESD by mechanism (no water involvement) but is documented as an OSHA-investigated marina fatality.
