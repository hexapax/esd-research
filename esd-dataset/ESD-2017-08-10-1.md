# ESD-2017-08-10-1: Cole Grad — Columbia River, Pasco, WA

## Summary

On August 10, 2017, Cole Grad, 15, was electrocuted at a pump station on the Columbia River near Pasco, WA. A cable fault energized the pump motor case; a wiring defect prevented the breaker from tripping. Two firefighters were also shocked during rescue.
