# ESD-2017-08-28-1: Vizueth & Perez — Houston, TX (Hurricane Harvey)

## Summary

On August 28, 2017, during Hurricane Harvey, a rescue boat carrying 7 men was pushed into downed power lines in Greens Bayou, east Houston. Two volunteer rescuers died, two went missing (presumed dead), and three survivors clung to trees for 18 hours before rescue. The group had saved 7 people before the tragedy.
