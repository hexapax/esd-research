# ESD-2023-07-17-1: Dr. Marshall Meadors III — Lake Hartwell, SC

## Summary

On July 17, 2023, Dr. Marshall L. Meadors III, 65, was found dead in the water at Lake Hartwell near Mid Lake Heights Drive in Anderson County, South Carolina. He had gone missing Monday evening. Investigators found exposed electrical wires on an electrical box providing power to the dock. The Anderson County Coroner ruled the preliminary cause of death as freshwater drowning possibly secondary to electrocution.
