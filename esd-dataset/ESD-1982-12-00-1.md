# ESD-1982-12-00-1: G. Brockett Irwin — Lake O' The Pines, TX

## Summary

In December 1982, attorney G. Brockett Irwin was electrocuted when his sailboat's mast struck an overhead power line crossing Lake O' The Pines near Johnson Creek in East Texas. Three other occupants survived. This was a precursor to the 2017 Boy Scout deaths at the same lake. Only one secondary source documents this incident.
