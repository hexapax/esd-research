# ESD-2017-07-23-1: Cameron Brooks — Spallumcheen, BC Canada

## Summary

On July 23, 2017, Cameron Brooks (28) attempted to rescue his two dogs from an electrified creek in Spallumcheen, BC. Both dogs died. Brooks waded in up to his thighs, was overwhelmed by current, fell backwards, and pulled himself out. He was airlifted to Kelowna General Hospital with leg burns. A faulty submersible irrigation pump had energized the creek.
