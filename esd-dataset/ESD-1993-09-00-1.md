# ESD-1993-09-00-1: Unknown Victim — Texas (Salt Water Boatlift)

## Summary

In approximately September 1993, a single drowning fatality occurred in Texas due to a fault in a boatlift in a salt water environment. No victim name or specific location is known. This is one of the few ESD incidents classified as occurring in salt water, which is scientifically unusual since salt water typically diverts electrical current around swimmers rather than through them. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
