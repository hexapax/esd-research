# ESD-0000-00-00-3: Two Juvenile Females — Lake Travis, TX

## Summary

Two juvenile females were electrocuted after jumping into the water from a dock at Lake Travis, Texas. Witnesses described classic ESD symptoms: rigid posture, inability to speak, then going limp. A faulty battery charger on a houseboat with no grounding system was identified as the cause. The date is unknown (likely pre-2010). No victim names or independent sources have been found.
