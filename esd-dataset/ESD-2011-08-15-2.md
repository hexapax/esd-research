# ESD-2011-08-15-2: Michael Knudsen — Clinch Marina, Traverse City MI

## Summary

On August 15, 2011, Michael Scott Knudsen, 18, of Mancelona, Michigan, was electrocuted at the Duncan L. Clinch Marina in Traverse City. Knudsen and friend Zachary Kott-Millard jumped from F-Dock into West Grand Traverse Bay at approximately 7:30 p.m. Knudsen immediately struggled, bleeding from his eyes, ears, and nose. Friends who attempted to grab his arms were also shocked. Marina employees cut power to the dock, and two firefighters found Knudsen unresponsive beneath the dock. Autopsy determined death from a combination of electrical shock and drowning. Investigation revealed a short circuit in a 220-volt line with corroded insulation in metal junction boxes (the original design had specified fiberglass), a failed circuit breaker, and deteriorated equipment ground materials. Prior marina users had reported feeling electrical current near the dock but the warnings went unheeded.
