# ESD-2023-02-07-1: James Stanley — Detroit, MI

## Summary

On February 7, 2023, James Stanley, 62, a plumber, was electrocuted in the flooded basement of Russell Woods Apartments in Detroit while snaking a drain. The entire building was electrically charged through 3-4 feet of flood water. Fire crews waited for DTE Energy to cut power before they could reach him.
