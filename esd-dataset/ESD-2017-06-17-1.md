# ESD-2017-06-17-1: Kayla Matos — Toms River, NJ

## Summary

On June 17, 2017, Kayla Matos, 11, was electrocuted in a Toms River lagoon when her inflatable raft contacted a dormant boat lift with corroded electrical equipment. Despite wearing life jackets and adults being present, she died at the hospital. The lift had been unused for years.
