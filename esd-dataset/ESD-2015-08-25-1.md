# ESD-2015-08-25-1: Joel St. Jean — Portsmouth Naval Shipyard, Kittery ME

## Summary

On August 25, 2015, Joel St. Jean, a commercial diver, was shocked while cleaning a sailboat at Portsmouth Naval Shipyard marina (Kittery, ME). Found floating unconscious with regulator in mouth. He suffered headaches, memory loss, and fatigue, and filed a $500,000 lawsuit against the U.S. government alleging faulty marina wiring.
