# ESD-2010-05-29-2: Unknown Boy — Lake Waccamaw, NC

## Summary

On May 29, 2010, a boy was killed by electrocution while swimming near a private covered dock with a boat lift at Lake Waccamaw, Columbus County, North Carolina. A grandfather had taken children to play in 3 feet of water under the dock. One child had reported feeling a "tingle" in the water before the fatal incident, but the warning went unrecognized. The grandfather found the boy floating face down and CPR was unsuccessful. Investigation revealed a severed equipment ground conductor in the house crawl space, a water-filled junction box beneath the dock, and the absence of required GFCI protection. The electrical work had been unpermitted and performed by an unlicensed contractor.
