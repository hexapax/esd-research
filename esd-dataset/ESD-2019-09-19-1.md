# ESD-2019-09-19-1: Hunter Morrison — Fannett, TX (Tropical Storm Imelda)

## Summary

On September 19, 2019, Hunter Morrison, 19, was electrocuted in Tropical Storm Imelda floodwater while trying to move his horse in the Fannett area of Jefferson County, Texas. The electrical source was never identified. This was the first confirmed death from Imelda in the county.
