# ESD-2014-07-20-1: Andrew Orvis — Lake Conroe, TX

## Summary

On July 20, 2014, Andrew Orvis, 8, was electrocuted at the dock of Piney Shores Resort on Lake Conroe. Broken conduit with exposed wires energized the dock. The Harris County medical examiner confirmed electrocution as cause of death, explicitly ruling out drowning as a contributing factor.
