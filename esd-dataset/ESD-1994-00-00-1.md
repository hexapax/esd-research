# ESD-1994-00-00-1: Unknown Boy — Bolling AFB, Washington DC

## Summary

In approximately 1994, a young boy was electrocuted at Bolling Air Force Base in Washington, DC, when he reached from the water and grabbed the support structure of an electrical junction box. Bare wires were found touching the metal case, and the ground wire had been cut. No victim name or additional details are known. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
