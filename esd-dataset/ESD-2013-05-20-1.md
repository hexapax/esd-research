# ESD-2013-05-20-1: Kyle McGonigle — Rough River Lake, Grayson County KY

## Summary

On Monday, May 20, 2013, Kyle Richard McGonigle, 36, of Vine Grove, Kentucky, was electrocuted at Rough River Lake near Peter Cave in Grayson County. McGonigle was with three co-workers swimming and fishing when he heard a friend's dog yelping in the water. He dove in to rescue the dog, and both he and the dog were killed by electric shock. Autopsy confirmed low-voltage electrocution as the cause of death. Investigation found two energized shore power cords had fallen into the water, and electrical inspection revealed multiple violations including spliced cords, non-functioning GFCI receptacles, and damaged equipment. His death was cited alongside other Kentucky ESD fatalities as a catalyst for the Samantha Chipley Act (HB 107).
