# ESD-2012-08-30-1: Jacquelyn Paulson, Michael Hicks, Preston Tarpley — Blackfoot, ID

## Summary

On Thursday, August 30, 2012, three people were fatally electrocuted in an irrigation canal approximately three miles northwest of Blackfoot, Bingham County, Idaho. Jacquelyn R. Paulson, 31, an Idaho State University assistant track and field coach and two-time NCAA All-American, went to search for the family dog around 9:30 p.m. The dog had entered the canal and was shocked by electrified water. Paulson entered the water and was also electrocuted. Michael Lance Hicks, 41, and Preston Keith Tarpley, 49, both of Blackfoot, then entered the water attempting to rescue Paulson and were also killed. The root cause was a broken ground wire between the transformer pole and the service pole, which energized the irrigation pump system and electrified the canal water. Circuit breakers failed to trip.
