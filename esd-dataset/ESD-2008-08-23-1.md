# ESD-2008-08-23-1: Sara Crotts — Lake Hamilton, Hot Springs AR

## Summary

On Saturday, August 23, 2008, Sara Crotts, 14, of Sherwood, Arkansas, died from electrocution with drowning at Lake Hamilton Resort in Hot Springs, Arkansas. Crotts was swimming with Tamara Keele, 40, and her son Devin Keele, 6, both of Sherwood, at approximately 8 p.m. When the young boy began experiencing electrical shock, Crotts attempted to help him and was fatally electrocuted. Investigation found 103 VAC on the metal ladder and an unbonded metal pole welded to the dock with a halogen light as contributing factors.
