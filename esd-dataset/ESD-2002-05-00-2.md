# ESD-2002-05-00-2: Unknown Girl (14) — Arlington, TX (Pool Electrocution)

## Summary

In May 2002, a 14-year-old girl was electrocuted in an apartment complex swimming pool in Arlington, Texas. Faulty wiring in underwater pool lights charged the water with electricity. A 16-year-old male who jumped in to rescue her was severely shocked. A third person used a fiberglass shepherd's hook to extract both victims. The CPSC cited this case prominently in their 2003 nationwide pool safety campaign.
