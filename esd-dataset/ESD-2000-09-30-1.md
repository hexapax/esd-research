# ESD-2000-09-30-1: Two Young Men (21, 22) — Tims Ford Lake, Winchester TN

## Summary

On September 30, 2000, two young men ages 21 and 22 were electrocuted and drowned after jumping from a dock at Tims Ford Lake in Winchester, Tennessee. The cause was improperly installed electrical work on a newly constructed dock that allowed a live wire to energize the surrounding water. A rescue diver also felt electric shock during recovery. The ESDPA list incorrectly records the date as August 1, 1999.
