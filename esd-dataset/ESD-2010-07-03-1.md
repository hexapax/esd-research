# ESD-2010-07-03-1: Four Women Shocked at Marina, One Revived by EMS — Friendsville TN

## Summary

On July 3, 2010, three females (ages 12, 20, and 30) were shocked near a docked boat at International Harbor, Friendsville, Tennessee on Fort Loudoun Lake. One victim was not breathing when EMS arrived and was revived. A 63-year-old woman was also shocked. All four victims were hospitalized and all survived.
