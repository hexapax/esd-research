# ESD-2012-05-28-1: Three Women Shocked at Dock, One Requires CPR — Lake of the Ozarks MO

## Summary

On May 28, 2012 (Memorial Day weekend), three women were shocked while swimming near a dock at the Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. Someone turned off the power. One woman needed CPR and was revived. All three survived. Investigation found no GFCI protection and an inadequate ground wire on the dock. This incident preceded the fatal July 4, 2012 double-fatality at the same lake by five weeks.
