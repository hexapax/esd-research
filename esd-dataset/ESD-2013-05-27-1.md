# ESD-2013-05-27-1: Gary & Ava Muter — Lake Lanier, Gainesville GA

## Summary

On Memorial Day (May 27, 2013), Gary Muter and his 6-year-old granddaughter Ava were swimming approximately 10 feet from a dock at Lake Lanier, Gainesville, Georgia. Gary felt a jolt in his elbow, immediately recognized it as electric shock, grabbed Ava and got both out of the water. The source of the electrical current was never conclusively identified; the dock's electrical system was ruled out by an electrician.
