# ESD-2018-06-11-1: Camelot Cove Dock Near-Miss — Lake of the Ozarks, MO

## Summary

On June 11, 2018, swimmers felt electric shock at Camelot Cove, Lake of the Ozarks. A bare energized wire from a neighbor's dock was dangling in the water. Described as potentially fatal but discovered before serious injury occurred.
