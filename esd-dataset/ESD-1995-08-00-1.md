# ESD-1995-08-00-1: Seven Children Shocked Behind Houseboat — Lake Cumberland, Jamestown KY

## Summary

In August 1995, seven children swimming behind a houseboat at Jamestown Resort and Marina on Lake Cumberland, Kentucky received electric shocks. All survived without serious injury. A loss of neutral in the electrical supply was identified as the cause.
