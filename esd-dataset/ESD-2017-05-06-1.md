# ESD-2017-05-06-1: Richard Lafontaine — Gatineau, QC Canada

## Summary

On May 6, 2017, Richard Lafontaine fell into electrified floodwater while building sandbag barriers during the 2017 Ottawa River flooding in Gatineau, Quebec. He was in critical condition after bystanders performed CPR. The floodwater was energized by electrical equipment (likely pumps) without GFCI protection.
