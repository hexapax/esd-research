# ESD-2021-06-04-1: Gustavo Delgado — Mission, TX

## Summary

On June 4, 2021, Gustavo Delgado, 14, was electrocuted while walking through storm floodwater near Mission, Texas. A submerged extension cord connecting a house to a trailer was the source. The cord was being used as a permanent (improper) power connection between buildings.
