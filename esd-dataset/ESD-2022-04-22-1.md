# ESD-2022-04-22-1: William Keith Elkins — Prospect Yacht Club, KY

## Summary

On April 22, 2022, William Keith Elkins, 58, a commercial diver, was electrocuted while working at Prospect Yacht Club on the Ohio River, preparing barges for Thunder Over Louisville. A stop work order had been issued two days earlier. State inspectors later found 33 electrical violations, and the fire marshal shut down the facility indefinitely.
