# ESD-2018-06-02-1: Smith Mountain Lake Boat Lift Near-Miss — Virginia

## Summary

On June 2, 2018, a 17-year-old girl was shocked after touching a boat lift cable at Smith Mountain Lake, Virginia (not Georgia as ESDPA states). She fell into the lake and was pulled out by another teen. Transferred to hospital with non-life-threatening injuries. Subsequent inspections found 96% of nearby docks had stray voltage.
