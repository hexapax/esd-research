# ESD-2007-08-28-1: Ryan O'Gorman Suffers Cardiac Arrest in Farm Pond — Eden NY

## Summary

On August 28, 2007, Ryan O'Gorman, 22, a recent Cornell University graduate, entered a private farm pond in Eden, New York to rescue a dog and suffered cardiac arrest from electric shock. His heart stopped for 20 minutes. His father, a physician, performed CPR along with a local police officer, and he was airlifted to Mercy Hospital. He walked out of the hospital four days later. The dog was killed. The cause was a submersible irrigation pump with deteriorated wire sheathing, installed before 1994 and never grounded.
