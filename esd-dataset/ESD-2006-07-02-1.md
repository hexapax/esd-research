# ESD-2006-07-02-1: Three Men Shocked After Prop Catches Extension Cord — Lake L'Homme Dieu MN

## Summary

On July 2, 2006, three men in a boat at Lake L'Homme Dieu, Douglas County, Minnesota had their propeller catch an extension cord powering a boat lift. All three were severely shocked upon entering the water. One victim was hospitalized for several days in serious condition. All three survived.
