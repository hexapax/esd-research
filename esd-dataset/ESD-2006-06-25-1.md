# ESD-2006-06-25-1: Jesse Fortner — Lake Ouachita, Royal AR

## Summary

On Saturday, June 25, 2006, Jesse Ryan Fortner, 14, of Saline County, Arkansas, was killed at Brady Mountain Resort on Lake Ouachita in Royal, Arkansas. Fortner and a friend were swimming near a houseboat dock when both came in contact with electric current. A boat owner was operating an ungrounded electrical cord, and the electricity found its way to a ladder leading into the water. Fortner's friend was hospitalized but released the same day. A bystander who attempted rescue was knocked unconscious. Officials could not definitively determine whether Fortner drowned or died from electrocution. His death inspired Jesse's Law, adopted by Arkansas in 2011, setting minimum electrical standards for boat docks and marinas.
