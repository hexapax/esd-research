# ESD-2006-05-22-1: Unknown Man (56) — Racine Harbor, WI

## Summary

On May 22, 2006, a 56-year-old man was killed while swimming from the stern of a boat at Racine Harbor on Lake Michigan, Wisconsin. An inquest ruled the death as accidental electrocution. A reverse polarity condition was identified on the vessel.
