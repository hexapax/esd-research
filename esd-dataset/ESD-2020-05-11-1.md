# ESD-2020-05-11-1: Meredith Defoe — Lewis Smith Lake, AL

## Summary

On May 11, 2020, Meredith Defoe was paralyzed by stray voltage while at her non-electrified dock on Lewis Smith Lake, Alabama. The electricity came from a neighbor's dock. Her husband rescued her with a pole. She became a dock electrical safety advocate and installed a Dock Lifeguard device.
