# ESD-2016-05-29-1: Unknown Girl — Silver Spring Township, PA

## Summary

On May 29, 2016 (Memorial Day weekend), an 8-year-old girl was electrocuted in a backyard pool in Silver Spring Township, PA, when a child flipped the pool light switch. She was airlifted to Hershey Medical Center in critical condition. Seven other children were shocked but escaped the pool. Final outcome is uncertain.
