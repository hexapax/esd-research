# ESD-2010-06-21-1: Unknown Man (54) — York, SC (Pool Electrocution)

## Summary

On approximately June 21, 2010, a 54-year-old man was electrocuted after jumping into a swimming pool in York, South Carolina, to rescue his granddaughter who was being shocked by the pool's railing. The granddaughter survived, but the grandfather could not be revived.
