# ESD-2015-08-08-1: Dr. Eric Hughes — Lake Tuscaloosa, AL

## Summary

On August 8, 2015, Dr. Eric Hughes, 37, a Tuscaloosa orthodontist, was found in 4 feet of water near his pontoon boat in Lake Tuscaloosa after going for a swim. Electric shock was determined to have contributed to his drowning.
