# ESD-2013-07-04-1: Daniel Petersen — Eagle Lake, MN

## Summary

On July 4, 2013, Daniel Petersen, 46, was electrocuted at Eagle Lake, Sherburne County, Minnesota, when he slipped while holding a plugged-in battery charger in 20 inches of water near his houseboat. He died July 7 at Hennepin County Medical Center. His sister was also shocked during a rescue attempt but survived.
