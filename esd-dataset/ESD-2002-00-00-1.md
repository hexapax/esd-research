# ESD-2002-00-00-1: Florida Divers Report Tingling While Cleaning Boat Bottoms — Multiple FL Marinas

## Summary

During 2002-2003, interviews with divers in Florida revealed many reports of high-level tingling sensations while cleaning boat bottoms in salt water. No fatalities or serious injuries occurred. The tingling was attributed to AC leakage from boats to through-hull fittings. This is an aggregate report of multiple incidents across Florida marinas, not a single event.
