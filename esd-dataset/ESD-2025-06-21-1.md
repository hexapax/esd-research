# ESD-2025-06-21-1: Unknown Boy — Blue Ridge Lake, GA

## Summary

On June 21, 2025, a 13-year-old boy from Fulton County, Georgia, was electrocuted while trying to exit the water via a metal dock ladder at Blue Ridge Lake, Fannin County. Two boys and a woman were also shocked during rescue attempts. The victim was a certified scuba diver. Numerous electrical deficiencies were found at the dock.
