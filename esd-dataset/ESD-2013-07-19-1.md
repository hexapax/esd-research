# ESD-2013-07-19-1: Paige Scheel — Syracuse, NY

## Summary

On July 19, 2013, Paige Scheel, 12, was electrocuted while filling an inflatable pool in her Syracuse, NY backyard. She contacted an energized extension cord or electric pump and fell face-first into the pool. A friend who attempted rescue also received a shock but was not seriously injured.
