# ESD-2018-07-03-1: Rocky Mount 5 Mile Marker Dock Near-Miss — Lake of the Ozarks, MO

## Summary

On July 3, 2018, a woman was shocked on a dock ladder at Rocky Mount, Lake of the Ozarks. Arcing occurred between the ladder and her rings. Several children also felt electrical sensations. Voltage measured at 3.5–15 VAC, deemed life-threatening. A faulty water heater was sending voltage through the ground wire.
