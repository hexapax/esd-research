# ESD-2017-06-16-1: Evan Currie — Put-in-Bay, OH

## Summary

On June 16, 2017, Evan Currie, 19, became Ohio's first reported electric shock drowning victim at Miller Marina on Put-in-Bay. After the family dog fell into the water, his father and both sons entered and were shocked. His mother unplugged shore power, but Evan was unresponsive. Remarkably, both the boat and marina power systems passed inspection — the fault source was never determined.
