# ESD-2001-04-10-1: Unknown Boy — New Orleans, LA (Shrimp Conveyor)

## Summary

On April 10, 2001, a boy was electrocuted while using an ungrounded conveyor to transfer shrimp in New Orleans, Louisiana, in a salt water / commercial fishing environment. No victim name or additional details are known. This incident occurred in a commercial context rather than the typical recreational marina setting for ESD. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
