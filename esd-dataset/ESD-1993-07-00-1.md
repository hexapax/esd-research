# ESD-1993-07-00-1: Two Teenage Girls — Alexandria Bay, NY

## Summary

In July 1993, two teenage girls snorkeling near a dock at Alexandria Bay, New York, in the Thousand Islands region of the St. Lawrence River, were paralyzed by electric shock and drowned. The dock wiring had been gnawed by rodents, creating the electrical fault. Reports indicate two bystanders who entered the water to help were also shocked. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
