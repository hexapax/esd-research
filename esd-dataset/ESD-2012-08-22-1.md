# ESD-2012-08-22-1: Irma Zamora and Stacey Schreiber — Valley Village, Los Angeles CA

## Summary

On Wednesday, August 22, 2012, at approximately 8:25 p.m., two Good Samaritan women were electrocuted in Valley Village, Los Angeles, California. Driver Arman Samsonian lost control of his SUV at high speed, crashed into a fire hydrant and light pole at Magnolia Boulevard and Ben Avenue. Water from the damaged hydrant contacted exposed electrical lines, creating an electrified puddle estimated at 4,800 volts. Irma Yolanda Zamora, 40, of Burbank, and Stacey Lee Schreiber, 39, of Valley Village, rushed to help the driver and were killed by the energized water. Approximately eight other people, including an LAPD officer, were also shocked. Samsonian later pleaded no contest to vehicular manslaughter.
