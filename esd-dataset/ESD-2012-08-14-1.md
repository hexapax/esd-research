# ESD-2012-08-14-1: Two Sisters Shocked in Backyard Pool, 15-Year-Old Revived by CPR — Garden Grove CA

## Summary

On August 14, 2012, sisters Bianca Cruz (15) and Tiffany Cruz (23) were shocked while swimming in their backyard pool in Garden Grove, California. Bianca became unresponsive at the deep end and required CPR from responding police officers. Their father Julio Cruz was shocked while pulling Bianca from the pool. Both sisters were hospitalized at UCI Medical Center. Bianca was expected to make a full recovery.
