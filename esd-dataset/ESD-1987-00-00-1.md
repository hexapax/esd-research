# ESD-1987-00-00-1: Unknown Male (18) — Bay Haven Marina, Lake Macatawa MI

## Summary

In 1987 or 1988, an 18-year-old male fell off a dock at Bay Haven Marina on Lake Macatawa in Park Township (Holland area), Michigan, and drowned due to electric shock in the water. Two would-be rescuers were thwarted by severe electric shock when they attempted to enter the water. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
