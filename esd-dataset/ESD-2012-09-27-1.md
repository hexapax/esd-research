# ESD-2012-09-27-1: Will Nolan — Tims Ford Lake, Winchester TN

## Summary

On September 27, 2012, William "Will" Nolan, 39, a professor of film, literary, and cultural studies at the University of Alabama's Honors College, was electrocuted while swimming at a private dock on Tims Ford Lake in Franklin County, Tennessee. Nolan was visiting family in Winchester and went for a swim around 7 p.m. His mother-in-law later found his clothing on the dock and called authorities, who found him under the dock. Investigation by the Franklin County Sheriff's Office revealed that the house had been struck by lightning in the recent past, damaging the wiring from the house to the dock and energizing the area with electricity.
