# ESD-2007-07-28-1: Unknown Woman (24) — Lake of the Ozarks, MO

## Summary

On July 28, 2007, a 24-year-old woman was paralyzed by electric shock while attempting to exit the water using a metal ladder at a private dock on Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. She fell back into the water and drowned. Several people had previously reported being shocked by the same ladder, and the dock owner was in the process of shutting off power but did not act in time. The dock power wiring termination was found submerged under the dock near the exit point.
