# ESD-1991-07-24-1: Unknown Male (50) — Northeastern Oklahoma Lake

## Summary

On July 24, 1991, a 50-year-old man was found unconscious on a boat dock at a northeastern Oklahoma lake, wearing wet socks and shoes. His son was shocked while attempting to revive him. An electrical short was detected in the dock wiring. The medical examiner ruled the cause of death as electrocution. This was one of five electricity-related deaths at Oklahoma lakes documented by the CDC (1989-1993). The ESDPA list incorrectly records the date as May 11, 1991.
