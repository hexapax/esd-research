# ESD-2023-07-09-1: Dr. Kole Nikprelaj — Wright Island Marina, New Rochelle NY

## Summary

On July 9, 2023, Dr. Kole Nikprelaj, 24, and his girlfriend Dila Bujaj were electrocuted by a live wire at Wright Island Marina in New Rochelle, New York, after finishing jet-skiing. Both were found unconscious in the water near an electrified boat ramp. Bujaj survived; Nikprelaj was placed on life support and died on July 30, 2023. A wrongful death lawsuit was filed in November 2024 naming multiple electrical contractors and the marina.
