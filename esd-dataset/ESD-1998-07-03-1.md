# ESD-1998-07-03-1: Man Shocked on Swim Ladder, Hospitalized 48 Hours — Lake Chelan WA

## Summary

On July 3, 1998, a 21-year-old man was shocked on a swim ladder while exiting the water at Lake Chelan, Washington. He was hospitalized for 48 hours. Rescuers who attempted to help were also shocked. All survived.
