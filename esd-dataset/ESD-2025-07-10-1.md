# ESD-2025-07-10-1: Gabriel Gonzalez — Marina Shores, Portage, IN

## Summary

On July 10, 2025, Gabriel Gonzalez, 21, was electrocuted at Marina Shores on Burns Waterway in Portage, Indiana, after diving in to retrieve a cooler. He experienced a tingling sensation and went under. His brother Francis was also overcome but survived. A similar electrical incident had occurred at the same marina just 4 days earlier. The family filed a $50-100M wrongful death suit.
