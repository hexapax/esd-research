# ESD-2004-06-19-1: Unknown Man — Lake Travis, Austin TX

## Summary

On June 19, 2004, a young man disappeared between marina dock sections at Lake Travis, Austin, Texas. Postmortem examination revealed Joule marks (electrical burn marks), leading investigators to suspect electric shock drowning as the cause of death. No further details about the victim or circumstances have been independently verified.
