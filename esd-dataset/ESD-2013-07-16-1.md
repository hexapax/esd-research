# ESD-2013-07-16-1: Lauren Cecil — Lexington, NC

## Summary

On July 16, 2013, Lauren Suzanne Cecil, 11, was electrocuted at Brookside Swim Club in Lexington, NC, when a city-owned power line connector failed, sending current through saturated ground to the pool's metal ladder. This is a downed power line incident affecting a swimming pool.
