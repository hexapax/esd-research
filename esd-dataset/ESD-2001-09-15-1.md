# ESD-2001-09-15-1: Unknown Victim — Bay Marine Boat Works, Biloxi MS

## Summary

On September 15, 2001, a person was electrocuted at Bay Marine Boat Works in Biloxi, Mississippi. The cause was reverse polarity from recent electrical work, and multiple people were reportedly shocked. The incident occurred four days after the September 11 attacks, which may explain the absence of media coverage. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
