# ESD-2006-07-24-1: Heather Huff — Lake of the Ozarks, MO

## Summary

On approximately July 24, 2006, Heather Huff, 24, of Raymore, Missouri, was electrocuted at the 81 Mile Marker of Lake of the Ozarks. She was paralyzed by electric shock when she touched a metal swim ladder on a private dock. Her father was present and family members pulled her from the water and administered CPR immediately, but she was pronounced dead at 5:02 p.m.
