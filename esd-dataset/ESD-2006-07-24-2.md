# ESD-2006-07-24-2: Tyler Howarth — Lake Lanier, Cumming GA

## Summary

On July 24, 2006, Tyler James Howarth, 15, of Gainesville, Georgia, was electrocuted at his family's lake home on Lake Lanier near Cumming, Forsyth County. Howarth was standing in the water working on a personal watercraft beside a metal dock when he apparently grabbed an exposed wire. He fell back under the dock. Two 15-year-old friends attempted to pull him from the water but were also shocked. His father arrived, jumped in and pulled Tyler from the lake, and began CPR, but the teenager was pronounced dead at Northeast Georgia Medical Center. The Tyler Howarth Community Fund was established in his memory, raising over $27,000 for community causes.
