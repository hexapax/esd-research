# ESD-2017-03-24-1: Two Victims (59M, 51F) — Monmouth Beach, NJ

## Summary

On March 24, 2017, a 59-year-old male fell into the water at a marina dock in Monmouth Beach, New Jersey, while boarding a boat. A 51-year-old female friend jumped in to rescue him, and both drowned. The rescuer-drowning pattern is a classic ESD indicator, but the brackish/saltwater environment of the Shrewsbury River and the cold March water temperature raise questions about whether ESD is physically possible at this location. Cold shock and hypothermia are alternative explanations.
