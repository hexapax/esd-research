# ESD-2005-06-24-1: Man Paralyzed on Boat Ladder, Recovers After Power Shutoff — Lake Ontario NY

## Summary

On June 24, 2005, a man was badly shocked and temporarily paralyzed while on a boat ladder at Lake Ontario, New York. Power was turned off and he regained feeling. Investigation found no ground wire in the dock wiring.
