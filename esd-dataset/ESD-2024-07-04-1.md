# ESD-2024-07-04-1: Jesse Hamric — Smith Mountain Lake, VA

## Summary

On July 4, 2024, Jesse Cyrus Hamric, 18, from Steamboat Springs, Colorado, was electrocuted at a private dock on Smith Mountain Lake, Virginia. A 20+ year-old submerged boat lift was producing 116+ volts. Two friends were also shocked during rescue. This was the first documented ESD death at Smith Mountain Lake and led to new dock electrical codes in three Virginia counties.
