# ESD-2014-05-00-1: Three Children — Florida Apartment Pool (Near-Miss)

## Summary

In May 2014, CCTV captured three children being shocked by an energized metal handrail in a Florida apartment complex pool. Two girls were paralyzed on contact, rescued by adults who were also shocked. All survived. The cause was unconnected ground wires in the pool's pump house. The widely-circulated footage became a key ESD awareness resource.
