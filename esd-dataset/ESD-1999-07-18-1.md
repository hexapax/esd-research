# ESD-1999-07-18-1: Unknown Young Man — Lake Mohave, AZ

## Summary

On July 18, 1999, a young man was disabled by electric shock and drowned while swimming to the stern of a houseboat at Lake Mohave, Arizona. The cause was a homemade shore cord Y-connector that had disconnected, energizing the surrounding water. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
