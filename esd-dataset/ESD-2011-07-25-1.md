# ESD-2011-07-25-1: Hannah Kendall and Jade Garza — Tampico, IL

## Summary

On Monday, July 25, 2011, Hannah Kendall and Jade Garza, both 14, of Sterling, Illinois, were electrocuted while detasseling corn in a field near Tampico. The girls were best friends working with a crew of about 70 others when they stepped into a pool of standing water that had been electrified by a lightning-damaged meter on the center pivot irrigation system. Jade received the initial shock, and Hannah was killed while trying to save her. Eight other workers were injured, one critically. OSHA investigated but issued no citations. Both funerals, held July 29, opened with the same song: "If I Die Young."
