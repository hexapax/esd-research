# ESD-2011-06-21-1: Woman Submerged 90 Seconds After Shock at Boat Ladder — Kanawha River, Charleston WV

## Summary

On June 21, 2011, a woman was overcome by electrical current while using a boat ladder at Lou Wendell Marine on the Kanawha River, Charleston, West Virginia. She was submerged for 90 seconds before being rescued. A grounded neutral in the shore power pedestal was identified as the cause. She survived.
