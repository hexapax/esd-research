# ESD-2003-05-00-1: Wildlife-Only Incident (Six Ducks) — Allatoona Lake, GA

## Summary

In May 2003, six ducks were killed at Allatoona Lake, Georgia, when a shore power cord fell into the water as a houseboat pulled away from a dock. The 125V plug at the boat end of the shore cord had been rewired by the owner for 220V service, with L2 connected to the ground pin and the ground lead cut and taped off, causing the hull to rise to line potential and energize the surrounding water. No human casualties occurred. The dead ducks were found floating nearby.
