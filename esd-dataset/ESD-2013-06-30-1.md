# ESD-2013-06-30-1: Kevin Short — Lake Cumberland, KY

## Summary

On June 30, 2013, Kevin Curtis Short, 34, was electrocuted at a private dock on Lake Cumberland when a frayed extension cord energized an aluminum ladder. In his final moments, he pushed his young son to safety. The coroner ruled the cause as low-voltage electrocution (110V) sufficient to stop his heart.
