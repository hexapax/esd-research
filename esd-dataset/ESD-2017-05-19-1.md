# ESD-2017-05-19-1: Carl & Lucas McGrady — Florence, AL

## Summary

On May 19, 2017, Carl McGrady, 65, was electrocuted entering his pool to clean it in Florence, Alabama. His son Lucas, 35, jumped in to save him and was also overcome by electric shock. Carl died that evening; Lucas died June 2 after 14 days on life support. Pam McGrady was also shocked but survived.
