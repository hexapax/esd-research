# ESD-2016-07-21-1: Construction Worker (24) — Dock Construction Site (Boat Lift ESD)

## Summary

On July 21, 2016, a 24-year-old construction worker was electrocuted when he contacted an energized boat lift while installing trim at a new dock. He fell into the water and drowned. This OSHA-documented fatality is mechanistically identical to recreational ESD, with the boat lift as the energized source.
