# ESD-2017-04-14-1: Shelly Darling & Elizabeth Whipple — Lake Tuscaloosa, AL

## Summary

On April 14, 2017, two UA School of Law attorneys — Shelly Darling (34) and Elizabeth Whipple (41) — were electrocuted at a lakehouse dock on Lake Tuscaloosa. They went to sunbathe on the dock and never returned. A deputy was jolted by electricity during body recovery. This was the second ESD incident documented at Lake Tuscaloosa.
