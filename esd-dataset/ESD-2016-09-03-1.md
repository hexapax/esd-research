# ESD-2016-09-03-1: Rachel Rosoff — Raleigh, NC

## Summary

On September 3, 2016 (Labor Day weekend), Rachel Rosoff, 17, was electrocuted at Heritage Point community pool while checking pool chemicals as a lifeguard. A failed pump motor combined with a grounding wire that hadn't been inspected in 37 years. The case settled for $6M.
