# ESD-1989-12-08-1: Unknown Male (32) — Northeastern Oklahoma Lake

## Summary

On December 8, 1989, a 32-year-old man drowned while rewiring a submerged water pump at a lake in northeastern Oklahoma. Co-workers found blood on rocks along the shoreline, and a lake patrol diver recovered the victim from under 25 feet of water. This occupational incident was one of five electricity-related drowning deaths at Oklahoma lakes documented by the CDC between 1989 and 1993, where 96% of commercial docks violated the National Electrical Code.
