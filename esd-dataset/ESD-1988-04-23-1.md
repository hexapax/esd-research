# ESD-1988-04-23-1: Unknown Victim — Oklahoma (Submersible Pump)

## Summary

On April 23, 1988, a single drowning fatality occurred in Oklahoma due to a fault in a submersible pump in a freshwater environment. No victim name or specific location is known. A subsequent CDC MMWR report documented five similar electricity-related drowning deaths at northeastern Oklahoma lakes from 1989 to 1993, where 96% of docks violated the National Electrical Code, establishing a pattern consistent with this incident.
