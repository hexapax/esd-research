# ESD-2017-06-21-1: Menemsha Beach / Crab Corner — Martha's Vineyard, MA

## Summary

On June 21, 2017, several West Tisbury third graders on a school beach outing felt electrical charge while swimming at Crab Corner, Menemsha Beach, Chilmark, Martha's Vineyard. The harbormaster confirmed the tingle. Power was cut and the area closed. The suspected cause was faulty grounding work from a contractor 5-8 years prior on the transient dock.
