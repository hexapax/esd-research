# ESD-1999-07-18-2: Unknown Boy (7) — Center Hill Lake, Smithville TN

## Summary

On July 18, 1999, two boys wearing flotation devices were found face down in the water behind a houseboat at Center Hill Lake (incorrectly listed as "Cedar Hill Lake" in ESDPA records) near Smithville, Tennessee. The 7-year-old could not be revived; the 8-year-old recovered. The flotation devices indicate shock-induced paralysis. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
