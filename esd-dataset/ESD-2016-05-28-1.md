# ESD-2016-05-28-1: Greg Subiszak — Wildwood Crest, NJ

## Summary

On May 28, 2016, Greg Subiszak, 34, was electrocuted in the Aztec Motel pool in Wildwood Crest, NJ. A teenage rescuer was also shocked. The cause was an electrical short in a light fixture near the pool. The pool had been inspected just 3 weeks prior.
