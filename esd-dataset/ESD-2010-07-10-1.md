# ESD-2010-07-10-1: Woman and Rescuer Shocked at Houseboat — Green River Lake, Campbellsville KY

## Summary

On July 10, 2010, Beth Waite was swimming behind a houseboat at Green River Lake, Kentucky when she was shocked and began drowning. Her boyfriend John Childress felt shocks from the boat ramp and called for help. Houseboat owner Bobby Gullett turned off the boat's power and jumped in to rescue Beth but was severely shocked by voltage that persisted in the water even after the power shutoff. Both Beth Waite and Bobby Gullett received emergency medical treatment. All three survived.
