# ESD-2023-10-22-1: Nate Davenport — Harbourside Place Fountain, Jupiter FL

## Summary

On October 22, 2023, Nathaniel "Nate" Davenport, 45, a Navy veteran and father of four, was electrocuted at the Harbourside Place interactive fountain in Jupiter, Florida, while rescuing his children from electrified water. Eight people required medical attention. The fountain had a documented prior electrical incident on July 10, 2023, when 15+ people were shocked and a visible wire was photographed, but the fountain remained operational. Jupiter Police released a 149-page incident report; the Town Building Department declared the fountain's electrical system unsafe. A wrongful death settlement was reached in March 2024. Davenport received posthumous Carnegie Hero Fund recognition.
