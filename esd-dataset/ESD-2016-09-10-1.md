# ESD-2016-09-10-1: Sandra Sharp — South Bend, IN

## Summary

On September 10, 2016, Sandra Sharp, 54, was electrocuted in the flooded basement of her home south of South Bend, Indiana. She was found unconscious in approximately 6 inches of flood water. Despite CPR restoring a pulse, she was taken off life support two days later.
