# ESD-2004-09-12-1: Ken Lutrick — Ross Barnett Reservoir, Ridgeland MS

## Summary

On September 12, 2004, Ken Lutrick, 16, was electrocuted at Main Harbor Marina at Ross Barnett Reservoir near Ridgeland, Mississippi. Ken was with a group of boys after church; they had been fishing and then went swimming. When Ken reached for the ladder on a boat that was leaking voltage due to a homemade shore power cord that had shorted to the houseboat's metal siding, he was electrocuted. His mother, Elise Lutrick, subsequently co-founded the Electric Shock Drowning Prevention Association (ESDPA) with Kevin Ritz, becoming a leading advocate for ESD awareness and prevention.
