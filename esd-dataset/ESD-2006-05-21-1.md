# ESD-2006-05-21-1: Jeremy Whiteside — Weiss Lake, Cherokee County AL

## Summary

On Sunday, May 21, 2006, Jeremy David Whiteside, 24, of Rome, Georgia, was killed on Weiss Lake in Cherokee County, Alabama. Whiteside and friends had been wake boarding on the lake and were returning to shore about 8 p.m. Friend Mitch Bowles was swimming toward the pier when he felt an electrical shock while reaching for the ladder. Thomas Ayer and Whiteside both plunged into the water to rescue Bowles but were shocked within 5 feet of the pier. When the electricity eventually stopped, others pulled Bowles onto the dock. Bowles and another man then dragged Whiteside from the water and administered CPR, but he was pronounced dead at Cherokee Medical Center at 8:40 p.m. Cherokee County Coroner Don Rogers ruled the cause of death as electrocution.
