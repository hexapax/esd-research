# ESD-2001-06-06-1: Unknown Man (Rescuer) — Farr Shores, Lake Hamilton AR

## Summary

On June 6, 2001, at Farr Shores on Lake Hamilton in Hot Springs, Arkansas, a girl was shocked after water-sliding into the water at the stern of a boat with reverse polarity and a neutral-ground fault. A man who attempted to rescue her drowned. The boat was unplugged in time to save the girl. This was the second of three ESD fatalities at Lake Hamilton. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
