# ESD-2006-08-00-1: Scuba Diver Nearly Paralyzed Touching Propeller — Racine WI, Lake Michigan

## Summary

In August 2006, a boat owner checking underwater with scuba gear at a marina in Racine, Wisconsin on Lake Michigan touched a bronze propeller and was nearly paralyzed by electric shock. The cause was identified as a faulty water heater neutral wire on a boat in the adjacent slip. This occurred at the same location as a fatal ESD incident three months earlier.
