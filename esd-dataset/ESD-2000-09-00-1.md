# ESD-2000-09-00-1: Man Paralyzed Underwater Rescuing Dog, Saved by Current — Niagara River, Grand Island NY

## Summary

In September 2000, a man was paralyzed on the bottom in 5 feet of water while rescuing a dog at the Niagara River, Grand Island, New York. A slight water current moved him out of the electrical field, and he survived. Investigation found that Romex wiring had chafed against a steel piling, energizing it.
