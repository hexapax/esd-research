# ESD-1999-07-00-2: Unknown Male — Lake Mohave, AZ

## Summary

In July 1999, a young man swimming toward a houseboat at Lake Mohave, Arizona, was disabled by electric shock and drowned. Only 17 VAC was measured in the water — demonstrating the lethal potential of extremely low voltages in fresh water. The cause was a homemade shore cord adapter that became disconnected, combined with an improper neutral-ground bond on the boat.
