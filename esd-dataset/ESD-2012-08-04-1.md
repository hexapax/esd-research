# ESD-2012-08-04-1: Couple Shocked at Dock Ladder, Swims to Safety — Gravois Arm, Lake of the Ozarks MO

## Summary

On August 4, 2012, a woman and her husband both felt electric shock while reaching for a dock ladder at the Gravois Arm of the Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. They swam away to a neighbor's dock and survived. This occurred one month after the fatal electrocution of two children at the same Gravois Arm location on July 4, 2012.
