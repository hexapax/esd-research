# ESD-2005-07-00-1: Diver Experiences Tingling and Pain, 40 VAC Measured — Brooklyn NY

## Summary

In July 2005, a diver at a private marina in Brooklyn, New York complained of tingling and pain while in the water. Investigation measured 40 VAC in the water and identified a neutral-ground short on a recently installed water heater as the cause. No serious injuries resulted.
