# ESD-2000-00-00-1: Unknown Teenager — Norris Lake, Lafollette TN

## Summary

In approximately 2000 or 2001, two teenage boys were behind a houseboat at Norris Lake near Lafollette, Tennessee, when one could not be resuscitated and died. A damaged power cable was found to be the cause. No victim names or specific date are known. No independent sources have been found to verify this incident.
