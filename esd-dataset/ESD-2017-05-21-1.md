# ESD-2017-05-21-1: Aldo Jordani Rojas — Laredo, TX

## Summary

On May 21, 2017, Aldo Jordani Rojas, 14, was electrocuted after stepping into a storm-flooded puddle containing a downed power line in Laredo, Texas. The ESDPA list incorrectly states two brothers were killed, apparently conflating this with a separate Fort Worth incident.
