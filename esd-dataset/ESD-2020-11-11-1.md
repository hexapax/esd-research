# ESD-2020-11-11-1: Mark Mixon — Bradenton Beach, FL (Tropical Storm Eta)

## Summary

On November 11, 2020, Mark Mixon, 65, was electrocuted in the flooded laundry room of his Bradenton Beach home during Tropical Storm Eta. A plugged-in dryer in 4 inches of standing water was the suspected cause. EMS had to wait for the power company to cut electricity before reaching him.
