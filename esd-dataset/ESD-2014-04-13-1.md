# ESD-2014-04-13-1: Calder Sloan — North Miami, FL

## Summary

On April 13, 2014, Calder Sloan, 7, was electrocuted in his family's swimming pool in North Miami, Florida. The pool light's grounding cable had never been properly installed, and no electrical permits or inspections had been done. His death led to the creation of the Caleb & Calder's Awesome Foundation for pool safety awareness.
