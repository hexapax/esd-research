# ESD-2016-03-27-1: Jim Tramel — Palm Springs, CA

## Summary

On Easter Sunday, March 27, 2016, Jim Tramel, 43, died after jumping into an electrified pool in Palm Springs, California, to rescue his daughter who was turning blue. Seven people were shocked at the family gathering. The pool's electrical system dated to 1963.
