# ESD-2012-07-07-1: Robert Stoen — Pokegama Lake, Cohasset MN

## Summary

On July 25, 2012, Robert Stoen, 22, of Owatonna, Minnesota, was electrocuted when an extension cord fell from a boat lift into the water at a private dock on Pokegama Lake, Cohasset. Eric Hancock (35) and Cheryl Hancock (57) were also shocked and hospitalized. This incident was part of the deadly summer 2012 cluster of ESD fatalities. The ESDPA incorrectly dated this as July 7.
