# ESD-2016-10-07-1: Shiloh IL Construction Worker Near-Miss (October 7, 2016)

## Summary

On October 7, 2016, a construction worker's concrete saw contacted an energized electrical line in Shiloh, Illinois, rendering him unconscious in standing water. A rescuer was also shocked. Two police officers extracted the victim using a canvas tie-down strap and performed CPR. The victim survived and the officers received a Life Saving Award.
