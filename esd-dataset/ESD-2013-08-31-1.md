# ESD-2013-08-31-1: Raul Hernandez Martinez — Houston, TX

## Summary

On August 31, 2013 (Labor Day weekend), Raul Hernandez Martinez, 27, died after rescuing his 11-year-old brother from the electrified pool at the Hilton Houston Westchase. Two electricians were charged with criminally negligent homicide for unpermitted, improper pool light wiring.
