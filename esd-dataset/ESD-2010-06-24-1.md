# ESD-2010-06-24-1: Two Sisters Shocked at Dock, Older Sister Critically Injured — Lake Travis TX

## Summary

On June 24, 2010, a 15-year-old girl and her 8-year-old sister were shocked near a boat dock at Lake Travis, Texas. The older sister became unresponsive and was hospitalized in critical condition. Shoddy boat lift wiring was identified as the cause. Both girls survived.
