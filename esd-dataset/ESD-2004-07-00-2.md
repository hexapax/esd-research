# ESD-2004-07-00-2: Man Shocked Near Generator-Powered Boats, Rescuers Imperiled — Sacramento River CA

## Summary

In July 2004, a man was shocked entering the water near boats running on generator power on the Sacramento River, California. Two men jumped in to rescue him, and one rescuer was also endangered. The generator was secured, ending the hazard. All three survived.
