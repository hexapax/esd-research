# ESD-2020-08-29-1: Khaleel Reynolds — Houston, TX

## Summary

On August 29, 2020, Khaleel Reynolds, 15, was electrocuted in the unpermitted pool of the North Villa Inn motel in Houston. A pool light had been removed from its housing, leaving live wires exposed. The pool had failed multiple inspections and was never permitted to operate.
