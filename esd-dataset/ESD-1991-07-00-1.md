# ESD-1991-07-00-1: Unknown Boy — Lake Hamilton, Hot Springs AR

## Summary

In July 1991, one of four boys in a canoe continued into an electric field near a dock at Lake Hamilton in Hot Springs, Arkansas, and drowned. The cause was broken dock wire insulation. This was the first of at least three ESD fatalities at Lake Hamilton, which also saw deaths in 2001 and 2008. The repeated incidents contributed to the passage of Jesse's Law in Arkansas in 2011.
