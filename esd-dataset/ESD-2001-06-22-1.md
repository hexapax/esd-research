# ESD-2001-06-22-1: Joseph Salute and Maxwell Casillas — Lake Montclair, VA

## Summary

On June 22, 2001, Joseph John Salute (15) and Maxwell "Max" Scott Casillas (11) were electrocuted and drowned after jumping into Lake Montclair near a pontoon boat at a private dock in the Montclair community, Dumfries, Virginia. The boat's battery charging system was crudely rigged with an unapproved dock outlet and a spliced utility cord plugged into the wrong outlet, sending 117 volts directly into the aluminum hull. A third boy (Kyle Fisher, 12) was shocked but escaped. Prosecutors determined the incident was a tragic accident; no charges were filed.
