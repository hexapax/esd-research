# ESD-2018-11-04-1: Paugus Bay Dog Electrocution / Owner Near-Miss — Laconia, NH

## Summary

On November 4, 2018, a dog was electrocuted jumping into Paugus Bay (Lake Winnipesaukee) at Laconia, NH. The owner suffered a powerful shock attempting rescue and was hospitalized for evaluation. The cause was a customer-owned electrical fixture near the dock. Utility company Eversource disconnected power to resolve the hazard.
