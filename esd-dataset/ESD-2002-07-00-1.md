# ESD-2002-07-00-1: Three Swimmers Rescued After Shore Power Shock — Allatoona Lake GA

## Summary

In July 2002, three swimmers were in distress near a houseboat at Allatoona Lake, Georgia. A bystander recognized the electrical hazard and pulled the shore power cord, saving all three. One person was hospitalized for several days. All survived.
