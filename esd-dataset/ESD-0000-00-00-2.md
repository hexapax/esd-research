# ESD-0000-00-00-2: Unknown Boy — Oklahoma (?) Community Pool

## Summary

A 10-year-old boy was electrocuted at a city recreational center pool after touching an energized vending machine. A damaged power cord and broken grounding pin allowed the machine cabinet to become electrified. The incident was documented in EC&M Magazine but the Oklahoma location stated by ESDPA cannot be independently verified.
