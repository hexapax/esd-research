# ESD-2004-07-00-1: Diver Feels Tingling During Dock Maintenance — Saguaro Lake AZ

## Summary

In July 2004, a diver performing dock maintenance at Saguaro Lake, Arizona felt tingling near a pontoon houseboat. The shore cable was disconnected, eliminating the hazard, and work resumed without incident. No injuries resulted.
