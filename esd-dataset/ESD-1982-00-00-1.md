# ESD-1982-00-00-1: Woman Unable to Release Dock Cleat Due to Electric Shock — Egg Harbor WI

## Summary

In 1982, a 23-year-old woman grabbed a dock cleat to exit the water at Egg Harbor, Wisconsin and experienced tetanic muscle contraction, preventing her from releasing her grip. Her hand went numb. A passerby kicked her hand free, preventing further injury. An open neutral was identified as the cause of the energized cleat.
