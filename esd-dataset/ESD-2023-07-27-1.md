# ESD-2023-07-27-1: Thomas "Shep" Milner — Lake Lanier, GA

## Summary

On July 27, 2023, Thomas "Shep" Milner, 24, jumped off his family's dock into Lake Lanier in Forsyth County, Georgia, and was electrocuted by stray electrical current in the water from the dock's power box. A neighbor who entered the water to rescue him also felt electrical shock, swam to shore to cut the dock power, then returned to pull Milner out. Milner was transported to Northside Forsyth Hospital and died the following day. Georgia DNR confirmed the electrical source was the dock infrastructure. The dock had been built within the past 3 years by a licensed electrician.
