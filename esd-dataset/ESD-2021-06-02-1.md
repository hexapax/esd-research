# ESD-2021-06-02-1: Trevor Cate — Clyde Lake, TX

## Summary

On June 2, 2021, Trevor Cate, 18, was electrocuted at Clyde Lake, Texas, when a disabled sailboat he was swim-towing struck an overhead power line. The broken line fell into the water. He died the following morning at the hospital, just days after graduating from Clyde High School.
