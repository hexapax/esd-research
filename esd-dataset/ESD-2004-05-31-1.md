# ESD-2004-05-31-1: Women Shocked Swimming Near Rented Houseboat — Lake Barkley, Grand Rivers KY

## Summary

On May 31, 2004, two women swimming near a rented houseboat at Lake Barkley, Grand Rivers, Kentucky felt strong electric shock. They swam away to safety. A relative also confirmed experiencing the same sensation. Disconnecting the shore power resolved the issue. All survived without injury.
