# ESD-2019-07-14-1: McKenzie Kinley — Citrus Heights, CA

## Summary

On July 14, 2019, McKenzie Kinley, 9, was electrocuted while swimming at her father's home in Citrus Heights, CA. She contacted a live wire on an unsealed pool light that was under repair. Four other children in the pool were unharmed. The home's aging 1950s-era electrical system was a contributing factor.
