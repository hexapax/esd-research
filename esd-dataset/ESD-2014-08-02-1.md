# ESD-2014-08-02-1: Madeline Roskie — Puyallup River, WA

## Summary

On August 2, 2014, Madeline "Maddy" Roskie, 18, died while inner-tubing on the Puyallup River near Tacoma, WA. An ungrounded irrigation pump on adjacent property was later found producing 280V to ground. Her death certificate listed the cause as "undetermined" despite strong evidence of electrocution.
