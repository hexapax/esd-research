# ESD-2008-06-13-1: Construction Worker — Ocean Isle Beach Marina, NC

## Summary

On June 13, 2008, a construction worker was electrocuted while repairing a floating dock at Ocean Isle Beach Marina in North Carolina. He was using an electric drill with his feet in the water, connected to a marina receptacle without GFCI protection. OSHA cited the employer for serious safety violations. This is one of the few OSHA-documented fatalities directly attributable to marina electrical infrastructure deficiencies.
