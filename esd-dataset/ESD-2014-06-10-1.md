# ESD-2014-06-10-1: Alec McQueen — Bullfrog Marina, Lake Powell, UT

## Summary

On June 10, 2014, Alec McQueen, 22, was electrocuted at Bullfrog Marina on Lake Powell after jumping into water energized by an amperage mismatch in an adjacent slip. His body was recovered two days later at 113 feet depth. The incident highlighted the danger of worn marina electrical outlets.
