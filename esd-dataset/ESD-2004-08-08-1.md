# ESD-2004-08-08-1: Unknown Young Man — Lake Travis, Austin TX

## Summary

On August 8, 2004, a young man in good health was swimming in the evening between two sections of a marina dock at Lake Travis, Austin, Texas. He disappeared underwater and was not recovered for two days. Autopsy revealed Joule marks (electrical contact points) on his right wrist, left leg, and shin, indicating electrical current had passed through his body. No toxic substances were found. The case was listed as suspected electric shock drowning under investigation. The Joule marks represent rare forensic evidence of ESD at autopsy.
