# ESD-2017-03-29-1: Alex & Isaiah Lopez — Fort Worth, TX

## Summary

On March 29, 2017, brothers Alex Lopez (12) and Isaiah Lopez (11) were electrocuted by a downed power line at Oakland Lake Park in Fort Worth. The lines had been down 12+ hours after morning storms. Alex stepped on the hidden line; Isaiah was electrocuted trying to help him. This is a downed power line incident, not classic ESD.
