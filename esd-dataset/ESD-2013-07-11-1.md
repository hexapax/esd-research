# ESD-2013-07-11-1: Lake of the Ozarks Near-Miss (July 11, 2013)

## Summary

On July 11, 2013, eight college girls were swimming at the 35 mile marker on Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. One girl's arm went numb and others were shocked while exiting the water at a nearby dock. All survived.
