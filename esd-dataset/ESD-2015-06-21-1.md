# ESD-2015-06-21-1: Marcus Colburn — Lake of the Ozarks, MO

## Summary

On Father's Day, June 21, 2015, Marcus Colburn, 21, died from electric shock drowning at a rental property dock on Lake of the Ozarks. A faulty junction box energized the water; the circuit breaker had been tripping repeatedly (a warning sign). Taylor Curley was also shocked but survived after someone shut off power.
