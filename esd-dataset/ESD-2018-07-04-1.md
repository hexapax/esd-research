# ESD-2018-07-04-1: Colucci Family Pool Near-Miss — Canfield, OH

## Summary

On July 4, 2018, Shelly Colucci felt a tingle in her pool in Canfield, Ohio and ordered her son out. Ohio Edison confirmed stray voltage in the water. The family installed a Shock Alert device and warned other pool owners about the danger.
