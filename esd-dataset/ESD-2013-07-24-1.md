# ESD-2013-07-24-1: Stephanie Diaz-Ruiz — Ellijay, GA

## Summary

On July 24, 2013, Stephanie Diaz-Ruiz, 19, was electrocuted in Ellijay, Georgia, after stepping onto wet ground near a storm-downed power line while babysitting. Current traveled 25 feet through standing water to reach her. This is a downed power line incident, not classic dock/marina ESD.
