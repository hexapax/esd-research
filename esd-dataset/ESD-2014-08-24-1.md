# ESD-2014-08-24-1: Sarah Grace Tellifero — Lake Bruin, LA

## Summary

On August 24, 2014, Sarah Grace Tellifero, 13, was electrocuted at Lake Bruin, Louisiana, when a malfunctioning boat lift motor energized a pontoon boat's metal ladder. Swimmers had previously reported tingling sensations in the water — a warning sign of electrical leakage that went unaddressed.
