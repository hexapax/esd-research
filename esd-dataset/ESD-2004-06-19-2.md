# ESD-2004-06-19-2: Unknown Boy (10) — Lake Waccamaw, NC

## Summary

On approximately June 19, 2004, a 10-year-old boy was electrocuted while swimming near a private dock with a motorized boat lift at Lake Waccamaw, Columbus County, North Carolina. The boy's grandfather had raised the metal boat lift rails above the water to create swimming space. Other children had reported feeling "tingles" in the water before the fatal incident, but the warning was not recognized. The grandfather found the boy floating face down and administered CPR, but he could not be revived. Investigation revealed multiple electrical failures: the equipment ground conductor was severed at a junction box in the house crawl space, a junction box beneath the dock was water-filled, and GFCI protection was absent. The electrical work had been performed by an unlicensed contractor without permits.
