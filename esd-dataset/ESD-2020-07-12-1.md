# ESD-2020-07-12-1: Timothy & Michael Miller — Lake Pleasant, AZ

## Summary

On July 12, 2020, brothers Timothy Miller (53) and Michael Miller (50) died from electric shock drowning at Scorpion Bay Marina on Lake Pleasant. Investigation revealed their own boat had an improper electrical modification that bypassed safety features, discharging current into the water. The marina's systems were not at fault.
