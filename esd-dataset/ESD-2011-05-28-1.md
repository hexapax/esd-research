# ESD-2011-05-28-1: Brian Harwick — Lake City, MN

## Summary

On Saturday, May 28, 2011, Brian Eugene Harwick, 50, of Lake City, Minnesota, was electrocuted at a family home on Lake Pepin. His dog ran into the water and swam over a boat lift that had an energized electrical track running underwater to the shore. The dog received a shock and began to sink. Another person jumped in first to attempt rescue but was also shocked and managed to exit the water. Harwick then jumped in to save his dog, was shocked and rendered unconscious, and went underwater. Friends and relatives pulled him from the water and attempted CPR, but he died at Mayo Clinic Health System's hospital in Lake City.
