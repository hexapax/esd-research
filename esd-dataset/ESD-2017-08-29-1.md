# ESD-2017-08-29-1: Andrew Pasek — Houston, TX (Hurricane Harvey)

## Summary

On August 29, 2017, during Hurricane Harvey, Andrew Pasek, 25, was electrocuted while wading through floodwater to rescue his sister's cat. A submerged live wire and an energized light pole created a lethal electrical pathway through his body. His last words were "Don't touch me, I'm dying." The cat was eventually rescued.
