# ESD-1989-06-01-1: Unknown Male (36) — Southern Oklahoma Lake

## Summary

On June 1, 1989, a 36-year-old man was electrocuted at a lake in southern Oklahoma while working on a houseboat. He contacted broken-lamp wiring connected to a 110-volt dock power line during a fuel pump installation. This incident is documented as Case 2 in the CDC's MMWR report on electricity-related deaths at Oklahoma lakes (1989-1993).
