# ESD-2014-07-04-1: Lake of the Ozarks 7 Mile Marker Near-Miss (July 4, 2014)

## Summary

On July 4, 2014, several swimmers in a cove near the 7 Mile Marker at Lake of the Ozarks felt electric shock. The source was 120V AC from a live wire shorted to an improperly decommissioned dock ramp approximately 100 yards away. The ramp had been electrified for an estimated month before discovery. All swimmers recognized the danger and exited safely.
