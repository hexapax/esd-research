# ESD-1986-07-29-3: Det. Jack Deuser and Wife — Harrods Creek, Louisville KY

## Summary

On July 29, 1986, Detective Jack S. Deuser (41), a 20-year veteran of the Jefferson County Police Department (Kentucky), was electrocuted when he dove into the water at Harrods Creek Boat Harbor to rescue his wife, who was in difficulty in the water. His wife also died. The cause was a faulty light switch and missing ground on a nearby houseboat. This is classified as a line-of-duty death. The ESDPA list incorrectly records the date as June 8, 1986, and the location as Lexington, KY.
