# ESD-2003-08-00-1: Child Reports Tingle, Houseboat Found with Dangerous Leakage — Green River Lake, Campbellsville KY

## Summary

In August 2003, a child reported a tingle near a houseboat at Green River Lake, Campbellsville, Kentucky. A marina manager investigated and found 4 amps of leakage current and 8 VAC hull potential — extremely dangerous levels. A neutral/ground fault in the boat's inverter was identified. The boat was rewired and the leakage was eliminated. No injuries resulted.
