# ESD-1993-07-27-1: Unknown Boy (13) — Northeastern Oklahoma Lake

## Summary

On July 27, 1993, a 13-year-old boy jumped from a dock into a northeastern Oklahoma lake and was immediately paralyzed by electric shock from a short in the dock wiring. He surfaced screaming, then submerged and drowned. This textbook ESD incident is documented as Case 4 in the CDC MMWR report. It occurred at the same lake system where four other electricity-related deaths occurred between 1989 and 1993.
