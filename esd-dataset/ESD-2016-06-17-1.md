# ESD-2016-06-17-1: Donna Berger & Randy Freeney — Chickamauga Lake, TN

## Summary

On June 17, 2016, Randy "Mack" Freeney (62) of Sale Creek, TN entered the water near a private dock on Chickamauga Lake and was incapacitated. His neighbor Donna Elaine Berger (34) of Hixson, TN jumped in to rescue him and was also incapacitated. Both drowned. Freeney's wife attempted to help but was pulled back. Freeney's son made the 911 call.

## ESD Assessment

This incident exhibits the classic ESD rescue pattern:
- Two victims simultaneously incapacitated in the same water near a powered dock
- Rescuer (Berger, a competent swimmer) unable to function after entering the water
- Proximity to powered dock infrastructure
- No electrical investigation was documented in any public source

The incident remains **SUSPECTED** because no electrical fault was confirmed or denied. The standard "no foul play" determination appears to have been made without any electrical testing of the dock or surrounding water.
