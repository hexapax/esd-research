# ESD-2017-08-05-1: Boy Scout Troop 620 — Lake O' The Pines, TX

## Summary

On August 5, 2017, three Boy Scouts from Troop 620 in Hallsville, Texas, were killed when their catamaran's mast struck a 7,200-volt overhead power line at Lake O' The Pines. Will Brannon (18) and Heath Faucheaux (16) died at the scene; Thomas Larry (11) died two days later at a Shreveport hospital. The power line was operated by Upshur Rural Electric Cooperative at insufficient clearance per NESC standards. A nearly identical fatal incident had occurred at the same lake in 1982.
