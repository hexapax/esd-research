# ESD-2004-06-05-1: Unknown Boy — Lake Wylie, Charlotte NC

## Summary

On June 5, 2004, a boy died from electric shock drowning at a houseboat on Lake Wylie near Charlotte, North Carolina. Two boys were swimming at the bow of the houseboat when they called for help. One boy on the ladder reported being shocked while the other boy in the water was not moving. A friend rushed to disconnect the shore power cord, and the father entered the water, but his son could not be resuscitated. Investigation found substantial wiring errors on the dock and boat, apparently performed by non-qualified individuals.
