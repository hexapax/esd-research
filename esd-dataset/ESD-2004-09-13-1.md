# ESD-2004-09-13-1: Tyler Deeds — Lake of the Ozarks, MO

## Summary

On September 13, 2004, Tyler Deeds, 22, of Olathe, Kansas, was electrocuted at Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. Deeds was helping to remodel a lake house when he was shocked by a dock cable and fell into the water. He could not be revived. His death directly prompted 2006 code changes governing dock electrical installations at Lake of the Ozarks, though subsequent inspections revealed that approximately 75% of the roughly 25,000 docks at the lake did not comply with safety guidelines.
