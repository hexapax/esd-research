# ESD-2017-00-00-1: Unknown Marina Owner (80s) — Michigan River Marina

## Summary

In summer 2017, a male marina owner in his 80s drowned in six feet of water at a boat slip in his own marina on a river in Michigan. His spouse described him as a "good swimmer" who had fallen in before without incident. He was found floating upright with the crown of his head visible. The CDC MIFACE report (17MI208) explicitly documented that neither the sheriff nor the medical examiner checked the water for stray electrical current. The marina had active shore power infrastructure. The absence of any electrical investigation means ESD cannot be ruled out as a contributing factor.
