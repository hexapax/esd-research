# ESD-2010-05-24-1: Nickolas Greiner Shocked Rescuing Dog at Dock Hoist — Clear Lake IA

## Summary

On May 24, 2010, Nickolas Greiner, 30, from Ankeny, Iowa jumped into Clear Lake at his parents' home to rescue a dog that was struggling underwater near a neighbor's dock hoist (boat lift). He was shocked and sustained burns and bruising. A family member recognized the electrical hazard and disconnected the dock's electricity. Greiner was treated at Mercy Medical Center-North Iowa in good condition. The dog died.
