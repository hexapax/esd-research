# Research: FI-1991-002

## Original Incident Summary
In July 1991, one of four boys in a canoe continued into an electric field and drowned at Lake Hamilton, Hot Springs, Arkansas. The cause was broken dock wire insulation. The incident is classified as a fatal incident with 1 death. It is listed as PDF item #108 in the "Electric Shock Drowning Incidents -- Marinas" document by James D. Shafer and Capt. David E. Rifkin (Rev. 8/15/2025). The classification notes indicate an approximate date (month only) and that the same lake was the site of ESD #70 (2007) and ESD #89 (2001).
