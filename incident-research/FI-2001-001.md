### Notable Observations

1. **The business exists and is still operating.** Bay Marine Boat Works (note: "Marine" not "Marina") is located at 151 5th St, Biloxi, MS 39530. It appears in Yelp, BBB, Yellow Pages, the Biloxi Bay Area Chamber of Commerce, and other business directories. The business was established in 1981 and incorporated in Mississippi. The owner listed in current business records is Paul Bodin.

2. **No OSHA inspection records found.** A search of the OSHA accident investigation database for marina/boatyard (SIC 4493) electrocution fatalities in Mississippi returned no matching results for 2001 or 2002. This could mean the incident was not reported to OSHA, was not under OSHA jurisdiction (e.g., if the victim was a boat owner rather than an employee), or the record is not in the publicly searchable online database.

3. **No news coverage found.** No newspaper articles, TV news reports (WLOX, WXXV, etc.), or wire service stories were found covering this incident. The date of September 15, 2001 is four days after the September 11 attacks, when national and local news coverage was overwhelmingly focused on 9/11, which may explain limited media attention to this incident.

4. **No court records found.** No lawsuits, wrongful death claims, or legal proceedings related to this incident were located. A case "Bay Marine Boat Works, Inc. v. M/V Gardina" exists but is unrelated (a maritime lien case).

5. **Name discrepancy.** The incident file uses "Bay Marina Boat Works" but the actual business name per all business directory listings is "Bay Marine Boat Works." This may be a transcription error in the ESDPA incident list, or the business may have previously used a slightly different name.

6. **No victim name identified.** Without independent sources, the name of the person killed and the names of the other individuals shocked remain unknown from public records.

## Corrections Needed

1. **Business name:** The incident file states "Bay Marina Boat Works" but the business appears to be named "Bay Marine Boat Works" per Yelp, BBB, Yellow Pages, Chamber of Commerce, and all other business listings. The name in the incident file should likely be corrected to "Bay Marine Boat Works" (though it is possible the ESDPA source intentionally uses a different form).

2. **Date cannot be independently verified.** The September 15, 2001 date comes solely from the ESDPA list and could not be confirmed through any independent source.

3. **All other details unverifiable.** The cause (reverse polarity from recent electrical work), the outcome (1 dead, multiple shocked), and the characterization of the victim as an "owner" could not be independently confirmed or denied.
