# Research: FI-2008-001 — Stonewall Jackson Lake Electric Shock Drowning (2010, not 2008)

## Original Incident Summary

Per the ESDPA-derived file (ESD #69, unlabeled in PDF), on August 23, 2008, a 15-year-old was shocked while touching a swim ladder, fell back in and drowned at Stonewall Jackson Lake, WV. The cause was reversed hot/ground in a shore cord. One death.

**CRITICAL NOTE:** The date in the raw file is wrong by nearly two years. All independent sources place this incident on **May 29, 2010**. No independent sources describe any separate ESD incident at Stonewall Jackson Lake in 2008.
