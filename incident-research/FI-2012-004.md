## Corrections Needed

1. **DATE IS WRONG.** The original file states "Aug 22, 2012." Multiple independent sources (ESPN, Idaho State Journal, Idaho Press, LocalNews8, KTVB) all confirm the incident occurred on **Thursday, August 30, 2012**, at approximately 9:30 p.m. The date should be corrected to "August 30, 2012."

2. **Day of the week verification:** August 30, 2012 was a Thursday, which matches all news reports. August 22, 2012 was a Wednesday and does not match the reported day of week.

3. **Victim identification:** The original file does not name the victims. They were:
   - Jacquelyn R. Paulson (sometimes spelled "Poulson" in obituaries), 31, of Blackfoot, Idaho
   - Michael Lance Hicks, 41, of Blackfoot, Idaho
   - Preston Keith Tarpley, 49, of Blackfoot, Idaho

4. **Gender order in description:** The original file states "Woman and 2 men" which is accurate. Paulson was the woman, and Hicks and Tarpley were the two men.

5. **"Faulty irrigation pump" is partially accurate but incomplete:** The root cause was more specifically a **broken ground wire** between the transformer pole and the service pole, which energized the ground wire between the service pole and the irrigation pump. This caused the irrigation pump system to electrify the water. The description "faulty irrigation pump" is acceptable shorthand but doesn't capture the full technical cause.

6. **Sequence of events:** The original file doesn't clarify the sequence. Paulson was the first to enter the water (attempting to rescue her dog), and the two men entered the water attempting to rescue Paulson. All three were electrocuted.

7. **Location more specific:** The incident occurred about three miles northwest of Blackfoot, Idaho, in an irrigation canal in Bingham County. "Bingham County Idaho" is accurate but incomplete.

8. **Dog also died:** The dog that Paulson was attempting to rescue also died in the incident.
