## Corrections Needed

1. **DATE IS COMPLETELY WRONG.** The original file states "Sep 27, 2012." Multiple independent sources (news articles, obituary, coroner's report via WAVE3) confirm the incident occurred on **May 20, 2013**. The date is off by approximately 8 months and even in the wrong year. This should be corrected to "May 20, 2013."

2. **Day of the week verification:** May 20, 2013 was a Monday, which matches the news reports that describe the incident occurring on a "Monday night." September 27, 2012 was a Thursday, which does not match the reported day of week in news coverage.

3. **Victim identification:** The original file does not name the victim. His name was Kyle Richard McGonigle, 36, of Vine Grove, Kentucky.

4. **Circumstances more detailed:** The original file says he "jumped in to rescue dog" which is accurate but incomplete. He was attempting to rescue his friend's dog that was yelping in the water about five feet from a boat. The dog also died.

5. **"Submerged shore cord coupling" is CONFIRMED:** The WAVE3 follow-up report (Source 7), citing the Grayson County Coroner and medical examiner's autopsy, confirms that a submerged shore power cord was the cause of electrocution. Two energized cords fell into the water — one from a dock power pedestal, one from a houseboat. Multiple electrical code violations were found at the dock.

6. **Location is accurate:** Rough River Lake, Grayson County, Kentucky is confirmed. More specifically, the incident occurred at Peter Cave in Grayson County.

7. **Alcohol was present:** Detectives found alcohol at the scene, which is not mentioned in the original file.

8. **Cause of death confirmed as low-voltage electrocution:** The autopsy confirmed both McGonigle and the dog died from low-voltage electrocution, though the preliminary coroner's ruling had been drowning.
