## Additional Notes

While this specific incident could not be verified, electric shock incidents involving generator-powered boats are a recognized hazard in the boating community:

- Portable generators on boats can create electrical hazards if not properly grounded
- Multiple boats sharing generator power through improper wiring can create ground faults
- Fresh water environments (like the Sacramento River) are more dangerous than salt water for electric shock drowning due to lower conductivity
