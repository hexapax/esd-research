## Corrections Needed

1. **CRITICAL: LOCATION STATE IS WRONG.** The original file states "South Bend, IA" (Iowa). The actual location was **South Bend, Indiana** (St. Joseph County). There is no city called "South Bend" in Iowa. South Bend is a well-known city in Indiana. The state abbreviation should be corrected from "IA" to "IN." All six independent sources confirm Indiana. The AP wire headline explicitly reads "Indiana woman."

2. **DATE IS WRONG.** The original file states "Sep 3, 2016." All independent sources confirm the incident occurred on **Saturday, September 10, 2016**, with death on **Monday, September 12, 2016**. The date should be corrected to "September 10, 2016" (incident) / "September 12, 2016" (death).

3. **Death was not immediate.** The original says "A women was found dead in 6" of water." She was actually found unconscious (alive), CPR was performed by first responders, a pulse was restored, and she was taken to Memorial Hospital where she was in critical condition. She was taken off life support two days later. The outcome (death) is correct, but the timeline is more complex than "found dead."

4. **Victim name and age confirmed.** Sandra Sharp, 54. Not mentioned in ESDPA data.

5. **Spelling error.** The original says "A women" (plural) when it should be "A woman" (singular).

6. **Dispatch time.** Emergency personnel were dispatched at 4:28 a.m. on September 10, 2016.

7. **Box fan causation is speculative.** Per WSBT quoting Assistant Chief Thompson, the box fan as the source of the electrical current was "a little speculative" as no electrician had confirmed it. ESDPA data presents it as established fact.
