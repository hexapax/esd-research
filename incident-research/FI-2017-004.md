# Research: FI-2017-004 — Cole Grad, 15, electrocuted at pump station, Pasco WA

## Original Incident Summary

**Date:** Jul 12, 2017
**Location:** Columbia River, Pascoe, Washington — old pump station
**Victim:** 15-year-old boy
**Outcome:** Fatal

**Summary from ESDPA list:** A 15 year old was killed while swimming with friends in the Columbia. The teens were gaining access to the river at an old pump station which was isolated by a wire fence and barbed wire. One teen grabbed an irrigation pipe and was shocked and fell into the water. He disappeared. Friends got help. Rescuers felt a tingle in the water and one was shocked by the same pipe. An electrical fault at the pump station is the likely cause of the death.
