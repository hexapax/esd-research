# Research: FI-1987-001

## Original Incident Summary
An 18-year-old boy fell off a dock at Bay Haven Marina on Lake Macatawa, Park Township, Michigan, in either 1987 or 1988. The victim was in great distress in the water. Two rescue attempts were thwarted by severe electric shock experienced by would-be rescuers upon entering the water. The incident resulted in one fatality. It is classified as an electric shock drowning and is listed as item #112 in the ESDPA incident database (Rev. 8/15/2025).
