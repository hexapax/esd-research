# Research: FI-1987-001

## Original Incident Summary
An 18-year-old boy fell off a dock at Bay Haven Marina on Lake Macatawa, Park Township, Michigan, in either 1987 or 1988. The victim was in great distress in the water. Two rescue attempts were thwarted by severe electric shock experienced by would-be rescuers upon entering the water. The incident resulted in one fatality. It is classified as an electric shock drowning and is listed as item #112 in the ESDPA incident database (Rev. 8/15/2025).

## Additional Details Not in Original File
- Multiple independent sources consistently date the incident to **July 1988**, which is more specific than the original file's "1987 or 1988" notation. No source reviewed lists this incident as occurring in 1987.
- The item number varies across different versions of the ESD incident list (listed as #30 in an earlier compilation, #68 in a later one, and #112 in the most recent Rev. 8/15/2025 version), reflecting the growing catalog of documented ESD incidents over time.
- The incident is consistently categorized under the "Electric Shock Drownings" section across all source versions, confirming the fatal outcome.
- No source provides the victim's name. This may be due to the victim being a minor (18 years old) and the incident predating modern online news archives.
- No source identifies the specific electrical fault (e.g., faulty boat wiring, shore power issue, or dock electrical system failure) that caused the electrification of the water.
- Bay Haven Marina is located in the Holland, Michigan area (Park Township is adjacent to the City of Holland in Ottawa County).

## Conflicting Information
- **Date discrepancy:** The original file lists the date as "1987 or 1988" and files it under 1987. All external sources that provide a specific date consistently state **July 1988**. No source corroborates 1987 as the year of this incident.

## Suggested Corrections
- **Date should be updated to July 1988.** All independent sources consistently identify this incident as occurring in July 1988. The "1987 or 1988" uncertainty in the original source appears to have been resolved by the more detailed versions of the same ESD incident list, which all specify July 1988. The file might be more accurately named FI-1988-001 or FI-1988-00X to reflect the correct year.
- **No other factual corrections are needed.** The location (Park Township, MI, Lake Macatawa, Bay Haven Marina), victim description (18-year-old boy), incident description (fell off dock, rescue attempts thwarted by electric shock), and fatal outcome are all consistent across the original file and external sources.
