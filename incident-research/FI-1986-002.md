# Research: FI-1986-002

## Original Incident Summary
A single drowning of a diver at "Petosky" (likely Petoskey), Michigan on July 29, 1986, in a fresh water environment. The incident is classified as a fatal electric shock drowning. It is listed as sub-incident (B) of item #113 in the ESDPA incident list (Rev. 8/15/2025), paired with a similar diver drowning at Grosse Pointe Yacht Club (sub-incident A). Information was relayed 3rd hand, making reliability uncertain. The source file notes the city name may be spelled "Petoskey" (the standard spelling).
