# FI-2004-001 — Research Notes

## Incident Summary (from source PDF)
A 19-year-old girl drowned at Scott's Creek Marina on Cave Run Lake in Moorhead, Kentucky in September 2004. The incident was caused by a submerged battery charger that energized the boat hull, with the boat lacking a proper bonding system.

## Conflicting Information
The only references found online point to a similar incident at the same location (Scott's Creek Marina, Cave Run Lake, Moorhead KY) involving a 19-year-old girl and electric shock from faulty boat electrical systems, but dated June 30, 2005 rather than September 2004. This 2005 incident matches many details but has different causation (A/C system rather than battery charger in some sources).
