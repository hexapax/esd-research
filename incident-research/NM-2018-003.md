# Research: NM-2018-003 — Lake of the Ozarks Rocky Mount Dock Shock (2018)

## Original Incident Summary

Per the ESDPA-derived file, on July 3, 2018, a woman was shocked on a metal dock ladder at Rocky Mount near the 5 Mile Marker on the Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. An arc was observed at her ring. 15 volts AC was measured. A faulty water heater was the cause.
