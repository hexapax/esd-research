# NM-2007-003

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | July 20, 2007 |

| **Classification** | Near Miss |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 0, Injured: 0, Shocked/survived: 2 |

| **PDF Item Number(s)** | NM #56 |

## Description

An adult male was immediately paralyzed at the end of a dock at Lake Arcadia, Edmond, Oklahoma. His wife kept his head above water from the dock. A bystander was also shocked. High water had submerged electrical outlets.

## Classification Notes

Paralysis at dock. Wife rescue from dock. Submerged electrical outlets from high water.
