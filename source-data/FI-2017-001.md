## Description

Oklahoma City, OK. Bricktown Canal. One man was electrocuted after falling into a canal in the city. He reached up to grab a light post to get out and received a fatal electric shock. Another man jumped in trying to help. He received a strong electric shock and was hospitalized (no status on the rescuer). Electrical fault and lack of grounding was cause.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: One man electrocuted (fatal) after grabbing an energized light post to exit the canal. The rescuer who jumped in was also shocked and hospitalized — status unknown.

**DATE DISCREPANCY (🔴 MAJOR):** ESD section lists Sep 1, 2017; NM section lists Sep 30, 2018 — a 394-day discrepancy despite descriptions being word-for-word identical. One date is clearly a transcription error. ESD section date (Sep 1, 2017) used as primary. Cause: electrical fault and lack of grounding.
