## Description

Double drowning of a husband and wife at Harrods Creek on the Ohio River near Lexington, Kentucky. Two dogs survived. A faulty light switch and missing ground on a nearby houseboat were found.

## Classification Notes

Double fatality (married couple). Dogs survived. Faulty light switch and missing ground.
