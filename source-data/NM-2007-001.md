## Description

An owner cleaning a prop shaft in the water at Franklin Lock Campground on the Caloosahatchee River, Florida was shocked. His teeth clenched. He saw blue sparks. He exited the water. The breaker had tripped.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Blue sparks visible. Breaker had tripped prior. Teeth clenching indicates significant current flow.
