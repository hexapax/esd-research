## Description

A 22-year-old entered a private pond to rescue a dog in Eden, New York. He was thrown back and was unresponsive. His father dragged him out and performed CPR. He was recovering as of the report. A submersible irrigation pump was the likely cause.

## Classification Notes

CPR required. Dog rescue attempt. Submersible irrigation pump suspected.
