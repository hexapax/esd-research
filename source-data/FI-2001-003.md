## Description

Two young boys died in the water near a pontoon boat at Lake Montclair, Dumfries, Virginia. Electric shock drowning was the cause. Battery chargers on a modified extension cord were found.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Modified extension cord with battery chargers.
