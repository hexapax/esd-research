## Description

Mandeville, LA. A 23 year old man was electrocuted when he extended an aluminum pole into the water around his sailboat at his dock. He received a fatal electrical shock and died. The cause of the electrocution is unknown. The water may have been electrified, or he might have made direct contact with an electrified metal object.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: 23-year-old man received fatal electrical shock while extending aluminum pole into water around his sailboat. Whether cause was ESD (electrified water) or direct contact with an electrified object remains undetermined.
