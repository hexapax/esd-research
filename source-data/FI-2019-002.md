## Description

Jefferson County, TX. A 19 year old man was electrocuted while trying to rescue a horse in the flood water aftermath of Tropical Storm Imelda. There was a lightning storm at the time. The source of the electricity was not reported.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: 19-year-old man electrocuted in floodwater while rescuing a horse. Tropical Storm Imelda aftermath. Lightning storm was occurring at the time — source may have been downed lines, equipment fault, or lightning ground strike. Source not identified in report.
