## Description

A man and his 6-year-old daughter were 10 feet from a dock at Lake Lanier, Gainesville, Georgia. The man was shocked in his arm three or more times. He grabbed his daughter and both survived.

## Classification Notes

Repeated shocks. Father protected daughter. Both survived.
