## Description

Lovington, NM. A teenager was killed while in the bathtub using a cell phone. The phone was connected to a charger plugged into a damaged, non-GFCI protected extension cord. It is believed she touched the frayed part of the cord and was electrocuted in the tub.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Teenager electrocuted in bathtub using phone on damaged extension cord without GFCI protection. Direct contact electrocution in water. Cause: frayed extension cord + no GFCI.
