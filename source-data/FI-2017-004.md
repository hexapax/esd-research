## Description

Columbia River, Pascoe, WA. A 15 year old was killed while swimming with friends in the Columbia. The teens were gaining access to the river at an old pump station which was isolated by a wire fence and barbed wire. One teen grabbed an irrigation pipe and was shocked and fell into the water. He disappeared. Friends got help. Rescuers felt a tingle in the water and one was shocked by the same pipe. An electrical fault at the pump station is the likely cause of the death.
