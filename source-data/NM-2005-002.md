| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|

## Description

A man was badly shocked and paralyzed on a boat ladder at Lake Ontario, New York. Power was turned off and he regained feeling. No ground wire was found in the dock wiring.

## Classification Notes

Paralysis resolved after power shutoff. No ground wire in dock wiring.
