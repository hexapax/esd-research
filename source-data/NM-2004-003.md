|-------|-------|

## Description

Two women swimming near a rented houseboat at Lake Barkley, Grand Rivers, Kentucky felt strong electric shock. They swam away. A relative confirmed the same experience. Disconnecting shore power resolved the issue.

## Classification Notes

Rented houseboat. Shore power disconnect resolved. Multiple people affected.
