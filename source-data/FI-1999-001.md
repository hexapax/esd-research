# FI-1999-001

| Field | Value |

| **Location** | Tims Ford Lake, Winchester TN |

## Description

Two boys (ages 21 and 22) died from electric shock drowning at Tims Ford Lake, Winchester, Tennessee. A live wire in the water near a dock was the cause.

## Classification Notes

Same lake as ESD #51 (Sep 2012) but different location/year. Double fatality.
