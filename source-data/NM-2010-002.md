## Description

A 12-year-old girl grabbed an electrical wire at Hartwell Lake, Anderson, South Carolina and went into cardiac arrest. She was resuscitated by her father and airlifted to the hospital. A grounding problem on the dock was found.

## Classification Notes

Cardiac arrest — resuscitated by father. Airlifted. Same date as NM #47 (Sep 5, 2010) but different location. Dock grounding problem.
