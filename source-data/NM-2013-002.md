# NM-2013-002

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | July 11, 2013 |

| **Outcome** | Dead: 0, Injured: 0, Shocked/survived: multiple |

## Description

Eight college girls were swimming at the 35 mile marker on the Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. One girl's arm went numb. Others were shocked while exiting the water at a nearby dock.

## Classification Notes

Multiple victims. Shock at dock exit point.
