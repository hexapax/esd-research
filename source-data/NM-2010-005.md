## Description

Three females (ages 12, 20, and 30) were shocked near a docked boat at International Harbor, Friendsville, Tennessee. One was not breathing when EMS arrived and was revived. A 63-year-old woman was also shocked. All were hospitalized.

## Classification Notes

One victim required resuscitation. Four people shocked total. All hospitalized but all survived.
