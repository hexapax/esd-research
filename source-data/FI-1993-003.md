## Description

Double drowning at Alexandria Bay, New York. Two teenage girls snorkeling near a dock were paralyzed by electric shock. Dock wiring had been gnawed by rodents.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Rodent-damaged wiring. Double fatality.
