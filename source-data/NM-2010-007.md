## Description

A man jumped in the water for his dog near a boat lift at Clear Lake, Iowa and was shocked. His family turned off the dock power. The man survived but the dog did not.

## Classification Notes

Dog killed. Man survived after family turned off power. Outcome: Dead: 0 (human), Shocked/survived: 1.
