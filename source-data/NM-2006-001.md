## Description

An owner checking underwater with scuba gear at Racine, Wisconsin on Lake Michigan touched a bronze propeller and was nearly paralyzed. The cause was a boat in the next slip with a faulty water heater neutral wire.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Fault from adjacent boat slip. Water heater neutral wire fault. Same location as ESD #75 (May 2006).
