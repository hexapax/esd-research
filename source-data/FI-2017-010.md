## Description

Laredo, TX. A young boy and his brother were killed when they stepped into a large puddle of water in a field. A storm had downed trees and powerlines in the area. One of the live powerlines was electrifying the water. The boys frequently played in this field near their home.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Two brothers killed stepping into field puddle electrified by a downed storm powerline. Children regularly played in this field near their home — tragic consequence of downed line not promptly secured.
