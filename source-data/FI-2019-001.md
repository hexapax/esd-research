## Description

Peoria AZ, Scorpion Bay Marina, Lake Pleasant. 2 brothers were killed in the water after the first started screaming when he jumped in next to their boat. Two others jumped in and were rescued and treated. Investigators found a modification to the shore power cable system which energized boat's underwater metals. Took 10 min to get the power secured for rescue operations.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: 2 brothers killed. Two others who jumped in were shocked but rescued and treated.
