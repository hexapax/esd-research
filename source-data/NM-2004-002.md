| Field | Value |

## Description

A man was shocked entering the water near boats on generator power on the Sacramento River, California. Two men jumped in to rescue him. One rescuer was also imperiled. The generator was secured. All survived.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Generator power as source. Rescuer also endangered.
