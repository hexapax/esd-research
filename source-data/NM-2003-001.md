## Description

A marina manager found a houseboat with 4 amps of leakage and 8 VAC hull potential at Green River, Campbellsville, Kentucky. A child had reported a tingle. The boat was rewired and leakage was cleared.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). 4 amps leakage, 8 VAC measured. Proactive detection by marina manager. Same location as NM #49 (Green River, Campbellsville).
