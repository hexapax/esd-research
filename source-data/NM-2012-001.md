## Description

A 15-year-old and her 23-year-old sister were in a pool in Garden Grove, California. The 15-year-old went face down and numb. Their father was shocked while pulling her out. Paramedics revived her. She was expected to make a full recovery.

## Classification Notes

Non-marina -- pool. CPR/paramedic intervention required. Full recovery expected. Three people shocked.
