| Field | Value |

## Description

A diver checking zincs felt strong "electric pulses" in the Connecticut River at Essex, Connecticut (brackish water). The diver backed away. The pulses were gone after securing boat power.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Brackish water environment. Zinc checking activity. Boat power as source.
