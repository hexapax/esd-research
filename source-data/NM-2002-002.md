## Description

Three swimmers were in distress near a houseboat at Allatoona Lake, Georgia. A bystander pulled the shore cord. All were saved. One person spent days in the hospital.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Shore cord pull saved lives. One hospitalized for days. Same lake as ESD #85.
