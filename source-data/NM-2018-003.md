## Description

A woman was shocked on a metal dock ladder at Rocky Mount near the 5 Mile Marker on the Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. An arc was observed at her ring. 15 volts AC was measured. A faulty water heater was the cause.

## Classification Notes

15 VAC measured. Arc at metal ring. Water heater fault.
