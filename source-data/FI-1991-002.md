## Description

One of four boys in a canoe continued into an electric field and drowned at Lake Hamilton, Hot Springs, Arkansas. Broken dock wire insulation was the cause.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Canoe entered electric field. Same lake as ESD #70 (2007) and ESD #89 (2001).
