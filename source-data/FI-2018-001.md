## Description

Dixon, CA. Irrigation canal. Two highschool boys were electrocuted while trying to rescue a dog who had fallen into the canal. Three boys and a girl, with the dog, were crossing a narrow bridge over the canal. The dog fell into the canal. The dog's owner and another boy jumped in. They reached up slow their movement and touched the bridge they were crossing and were electrocuted. The other boy jumped in and broke the 2 boys from their grasp and also saved the dog. It is unknown if he got shocked as well. Investigation by authorities in progress. Something energized the bridge, and a lack of ground likely prevented a breaker trip.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Two high school boys electrocuted — grabbed electrified bridge while in water. Third boy jumped in, freed the two boys from their grasp, saved the dog, and survived. Something energized the canal bridge; lack of ground prevented breaker trip. Heroic rescue by the third boy.
