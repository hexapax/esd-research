## Description

A man attempting a rescue drowned at Farr Shores on Lake Hamilton, Hot Springs, Arkansas. A girl was shocked but saved. Reverse polarity and a neutral-ground fault on the boat were found.

## Classification Notes

Rescuer became the fatality. Reverse polarity + neutral-ground fault.
