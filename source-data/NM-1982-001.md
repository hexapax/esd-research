## Description

A 23-year-old woman grabbed a dock cleat to exit the water at Egg Harbor, Wisconsin and couldn't let go. Her hand went numb. A passerby kicked her hand free. No injuries resulted. An open neutral was found.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (year only). Passerby intervention prevented potential fatality. Open neutral. "Can't let go" effect from electrical current through muscles.
