## Description

Lake Clyde, Clyde TX. An 18-year-old boy was killed when the sailboat he was swim towing struck a power line. The line broke and fell in the water electrifying the water near the boat. Could have been an electrocution or an electric shock drowning. Power crews secured power to the line before retrieving the boy.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: 18-year-old boy killed. Sailboat he was swim-towing struck a power line; the downed energized line fell in water. Whether cause was direct electrocution or ESD paralysis-drowning is uncertain per the source document. Power had to be secured before retrieval.
