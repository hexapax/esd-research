## Description

A woman was overcome by electrical current in the water via a boat ladder at Lou Wendell Marine on the Kanawha River, Charleston, West Virginia. She was submerged for 90 seconds before being rescued. A grounded neutral in the pedestal was the cause.

## Classification Notes

90-second submersion — near-fatal. Grounded neutral in pedestal. Marina context.
