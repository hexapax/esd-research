## Description

An 8-year-old boy was tubing in a marina on the Multnomah Channel, Portland, Oregon and was diagnosed as electrocuted. An AC to DC short on a boat was found with 84 VAC measured behind the stern.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). AC-to-DC short circuit. 84 VAC measured.
