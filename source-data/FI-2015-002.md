## Description

Lake Tuscaloosa, AL. A 37 year old man went swimming from his pontoon boat after cutting grass. He was found on the bottom near the boat in 4 feet of water. Investigators say he was shocked by electricity before he drowned.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: 37-year-old man found dead at bottom of lake near pontoon boat; investigators determined he was shocked by electricity before drowning. Classic ESD scenario.
