## Description

A man felt electric shock and paralysis while swimming off a docked boat at Lake Norman, North Carolina. He managed to move far enough away to regain control of his muscles.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Classic ESD near-miss -- paralysis followed by escape.
