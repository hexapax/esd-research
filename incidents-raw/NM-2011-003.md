## Description

A dog died in a retention pond fountain in Houston, Texas. A man felt strong current at ankle depth and retreated. Power was secured.

## Classification Notes

Retention pond fountain. Dog killed. Man detected current and retreated safely. Outcome: Dead: 0 (human), Shocked/survived: 1.
