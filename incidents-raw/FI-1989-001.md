## Description

32-year-old male drowned at Lake A, northeastern Oklahoma. Found with blood on rocks along the shoreline; body recovered from 25 feet of water. Was working on submerged water pump electrical installation. Fresh water environment.

## Classification Notes

CDC MMWR Vol. 45, No. 26 (1996), Case 1 confirms date as **December 8, 1989** and victim as 32-year-old male working on submerged pump installation. ESDPA describes cause as "dock wiring fault" — CDC describes mechanism as "submerged water pump electrical installation." Same incident (Lake A NE Oklahoma, Dec 1989) — no duplicate.
