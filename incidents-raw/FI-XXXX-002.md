## Description

A 44-year-old diver disappeared while approaching a swim platform on the St Croix River, Prescott, Wisconsin. A battery charger had faulted. No AC grounding wire was present.

Date confirmed as **June 8, 1986** by OnTime59.com compiled list and AuGres Museum records (secondary sources). Battery charger fault. No AC grounding. ESDPA lists "Date Unknown."
