| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | August 8, 2010 |
| **Location** | Green River Lake, Campbellsville KY |
| **Classification** | Near Miss |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 0, Injured: 0, Shocked/survived: 2 |
| **Source Section** | Near Misses |
| **PDF Item Number(s)** | NM #49 |

## Description

A woman was shocked behind a houseboat at Green River Lake, Campbellsville, Kentucky. A man was shocked trying to help her. Both were pulled out with a rope after turning off the boat power. Both were treated and released.

## Classification Notes

Both victims treated and released. Rope rescue after power shutoff.
