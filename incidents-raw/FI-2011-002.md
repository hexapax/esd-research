## Description

**Michael Scott Knudsen**, age 18, died in the West Arm of Grand Traverse Bay at Clinch Park Marina, Traverse City, Michigan. Cause: dock wiring fault. A $50 million wrongful death lawsuit was filed; Michigan Court of Appeals case COA 304695 documents the litigation.
