## Description

A 21-year-old was shocked on a swim ladder while exiting the water at Lake Chelan, Chelan, Washington. He spent 48 hours in the hospital. Rescuers were also shocked.

## Classification Notes

48-hour hospitalization. Multiple people shocked including rescuers.
