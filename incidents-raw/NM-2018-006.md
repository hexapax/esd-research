## Description

A man's grandson was shocked at Lake Taney Como, Branson, Missouri. The cause was faulty electrical wiring THREE docks away from the victim's location.

## Classification Notes

Electrical fault 3 docks away -- demonstrates long-range propagation of electricity through water.
