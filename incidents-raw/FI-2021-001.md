## Description

**William Keith Elkins**, age 58, diver, killed at Prospect Yacht Club, Ohio River, Louisville area (Jefferson County) KY. Elkins was diving near a barge being readied for a local festival. The coroner confirmed electricity in the water. The Kentucky Fire Marshal subsequently investigated and shut down Prospect Yacht Club. OSHA declined jurisdiction; USCG and KY Labor also declined to take enforcement action. Source of energization not publicly identified.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: William Keith Elkins, 58, killed. KY Coroner confirmed electricity in water; KY Fire Marshal shut down Prospect Yacht Club following the death — demonstrating a regulatory gap (no single agency claimed jurisdiction over the fatality investigation).

**DATE CORRECTION:** ESDPA lists July 4, 2021. WAVE3 news and KY Coroner records confirm the actual date is approximately **April 20, 2022** (~9-month discrepancy). The July 4 date may refer to an earlier unrelated incident at the same marina, or may be a transcription error. Sources: WAVE3, KY Coroner, KY Fire Marshal.
