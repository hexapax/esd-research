## Description

46-year-old male scuba diver drowned at Lake A, northeastern Oklahoma. A fault in a submersible pump was found. Fresh water environment.

## Classification Notes

Submersible pump fault. Scuba diver.

**DATE CORRECTION:** ESDPA lists "August 1993." CDC MMWR Vol. 45, No. 26 (1996), Case 5 confirms date as **September 5, 1993**. Same incident (46yo male, submersible pump, Lake A NE Oklahoma) — no duplicate.
