## Description

A dog died at the lake in Perrine Wayside Dog Park, Miami, Florida. A man was stopped by shocks. The fountain was still electrifying the water after being turned off. It was removed for inspection.

## Classification Notes

Fountain continued energizing water after shutoff. Dog killed. Man survived. Outcome: Dead: 0 (human), Shocked/survived: 1.
