## Description

**Jesse Hamric**, age 18, of Steamboat Springs, Colorado (recent high school graduate), jumped from a dock into Smith Mountain Lake, Huddleston (Bedford County), VA. Hamric became unresponsive in the water; two bystanders who jumped in to help reported feeling shocked. Stray voltage from the dock's electric boat lift was suspected. Hamric died; the two rescuers survived.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Jesse Hamric, 18, perished. Two rescuers felt electrical shock but escaped the water (survived). Source of stray voltage: electric boat lift on dock. Investigation ongoing at time of document revision; medical examiner conclusions not yet public.

**VICTIM NAME:** Jesse Hamric confirmed by Cardinal News (Cardinal News, August 7, 2024: "Stray voltage dangers back in spotlight following Smith Mountain Lake death"). A 2019–2020 attempt to modify Virginia's building code for dock electrical safety failed due to industry opposition; no state law has been enacted as of 2025.
