## Description

Smith Mountain Lake, Huddleston, VA. An 18yo man jumped into the water at the lake. After recognizing the man was in distress, 2 others jumped in to help. They felt the electrical shock and were able to get out of the water. The 18yo perished from the electric shock drowning. Details pending.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: 18-year-old man perished. Two others who jumped in felt electrical shock but escaped the water (survived). Investigation details pending at time of document revision. Dates match across both sections — no discrepancy.
