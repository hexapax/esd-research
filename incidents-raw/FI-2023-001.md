## Description

Detroit, MI. Man electrocuted while working in a flooded home basement. He was snaking a drain when flood water poured in and filled the basement. The electrically charged water caused the death. The exact source is unknown. Took 45 min for EMS to get the power off to retrieve the man.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Man died from electrocution in electrically charged floodwater. Exact electrical source unknown. Notable: 45-minute delay for EMS to secure power illustrates the danger of electrically charged water in enclosed spaces. Similar incident occurred Aug 16, 2022 in Detroit (see FI-2022-001.md).
