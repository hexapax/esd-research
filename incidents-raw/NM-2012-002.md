| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | August 4, 2012 |
| **Location** | Gravois Arm, Lake of the Ozarks MO |
| **Classification** | Near Miss |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 0, Injured: 0, Shocked/survived: 2 |
| **Source Section** | Near Misses |
| **PDF Item Number(s)** | NM #39 |

## Description

A woman and her husband both confirmed shock while reaching for a dock ladder at Gravois Arm on the Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. They swam away to a neighbor's dock and survived.

## Classification Notes

Both shocked at ladder. Escaped by swimming to different dock.
