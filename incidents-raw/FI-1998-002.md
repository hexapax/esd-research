## Description

Two boys (ages 8-10) drowned at Rio Vista, California. Earlier, boys had reported a tingle and got out of the water. Forty-year-old faulty wiring under moored boats was the cause.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). 40-year-old wiring. Earlier warning signs (tingle) reported but not acted upon.
