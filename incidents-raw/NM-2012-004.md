## Description

Three women were shocked while swimming near a dock at the Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri. Someone turned off the power. One woman needed CPR. All survived. No GFCI was present and the ground wire was inadequate.

## Classification Notes

CPR required for one victim. No GFCI. Inadequate ground wire.
