## Description

An owner was paralyzed on the bottom in 5 feet of water while rescuing a dog at the Niagara River, Grand Island, New York. A slight current moved him and he survived. Romex wiring had chafed and energized a steel piling.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Paralyzed on bottom — survived due to current moving him. Romex chafing on steel piling. Dog rescue context.
