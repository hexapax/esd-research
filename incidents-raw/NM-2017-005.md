## Description

A man fell in a flooded street in Ottawa, Canada and contacted a cord powering a submersible pump. He was shocked and hospitalized. No GFCI protection was in place.

## Classification Notes

International incident (Canada). Flooded street/submersible pump. No GFCI. Hospitalized but survived.
