## Description

**Carmen Elizabeth Johnson**, age 15, drowned at Smith Lake, Priceville, Winston County, Alabama. Two teenage girls entered the water from a private dock; both were getting shocked. Johnson drowned; the other treated and released. The victim's father and his son jumped in to assist; the father blacked out. Power was cut at the house, after which he revived and survived along with son. Cause: missing ground and faulty dock light switch.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Carmen Elizabeth Johnson, 15, drowned from electrical shock. Second teenage girl treated and released (survived). Father blacked out in electrified water but survived after house power was cut; son also survived.

**DATE CORRECTION:** ESDPA ESD section lists Mar 27, 2016; NM section lists Apr 16, 2016. CBS News and Alabama Law Enforcement Agency Marine Patrol records confirm **April 16, 2016** as the correct date. NM section's date and spelling ('Priceville') are both correct; ESD section has a transcription error. Victim name from CBS News, ALEA Marine Patrol.
