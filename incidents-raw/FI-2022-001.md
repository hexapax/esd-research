## Description

Detroit, MI. Man electrocuted while working in a flooded home basement. He was snaking a drain when flood water poured in and filled the basement. The electrically charged water caused the death. The exact source is unknown. Took 45 min for EMS to get the power off to retrieve the man.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Man died from electrocution in electrically charged floodwater. Exact electrical source unknown. Notable: 45-minute EMS delay to secure power. Nearly identical circumstances to FI-2023-001.md (Feb 7, 2023, Detroit) — same city, same scenario, different dates.
