## Description

Florence, AL. A man and his son were killed in a swimming pool. The man in his pool for cleaning was found submerged by his son who jumped in to rescue. The son was also overcome by electric shock and later died. The wife was also injured by electrical shock trying to help the son. Exact cause is unknown.

*NM section adds:* A woman was injured by electrical shock when trying to help her son who went into swimming pool to rescue his father. The father and son both died. Cause is unknown.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Man and son both died from electric shock in pool. Wife/woman was injured (survived) trying to help.

**DATE DISCREPANCY (🟢 Minor):** ESD section lists Apr 14, 2017; NM section lists May 19, 2017 — 35-day gap. Descriptions are consistent. NM section notes woman's survival (justifying NM cross-reference). Cause unknown in both sections.
