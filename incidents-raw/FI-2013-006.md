## Description

**Daniel Petersen**, age 46 (from Big Lake MN), immersed plugged-in battery charger while standing in water at Eagle Lake, Orrock Township, Sherburne County MN; died July 7.

## Classification Notes

Listed in Electric Shock Drownings section only.

**DATE CORRECTION:** ESDPA lists June 30, 2013. Sherburne County Sheriff's Office confirms incident date as **July 4, 2013**; victim died July 7 (matches ESDPA). Some news sources (CBS Minnesota) report the victim's hometown of Big Lake as the incident location — the actual lake is Eagle Lake (Orrock Township). Victim name confirmed by Sherburne County Sheriff.
