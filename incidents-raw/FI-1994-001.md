# FI-1994-001

| **PDF Item Number(s)** | #103 |

## Description

A young boy grabbed the support structure of an electrical junction box at Bolling Air Force Base, Washington, DC and received a lethal electric shock. Bare wires were found and the ground wire had been cut.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date. Military base. Ground wire intentionally or negligently cut. Bare wires exposed.
