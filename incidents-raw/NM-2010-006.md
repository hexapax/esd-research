## Description

A 15-year-old girl and her 8-year-old sister were shocked near a boat dock at Lake Travis, Texas. The older sister was unresponsive and in critical condition in the hospital. Shoddy boat lift wiring was the cause. Both girls survived.

## Classification Notes

Critical condition but survived. Shoddy boat lift wiring. Both sisters survived.
