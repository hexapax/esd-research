## Description

Double drowning of a mother and daughter at Lake Cumberland, Monticello, Kentucky. A 125V plug had been rewired for 220V with the ground wire cut off.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Dangerous rewiring -- 125V plug rewired for 220V, ground removed.
