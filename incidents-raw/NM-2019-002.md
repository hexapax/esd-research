| Field | Value |

## Description

Numerous staff and guests were shocked at Universal Studios Volcano Bay water park in Orlando, Florida. No injuries were found after examination. A water coaster short circuit was the cause.

## Classification Notes

Major commercial water park. No injuries despite multiple people shocked. Short circuit in ride equipment.
