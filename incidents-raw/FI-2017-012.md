## Description

**Shelly Darling**, age 34, and **Elizabeth Whipple**, age 41 (both University of Alabama School of Law staff), went missing after sunbathing on a dock at Lake Tuscaloosa, Alabama. Both bodies were recovered the following morning near the dock. At least one investigator was shocked during the initial scene examination. Tuscaloosa Police identified the cause as electrocution from faulty dock wiring sending current through the water. Deaths ruled accidental.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Shelly Darling, 34, and Elizabeth Whipple, 41, both killed. Classic dock-ESD: both in water off the dock when current from dock wiring entered the water; investigator shocked on the dock during scene exam confirms electrical hazard was present at dock level. Tuscaloosa Police Department investigation.

**DATE CORRECTION:** ESDPA lists March 29, 2017. ABC 33/40 and Tuscaloosa Police Department records confirm date as **April 16, 2017**. Victim names and institutional affiliations confirmed by news reporting.
