## Description

Lake Tuscaloosa, AL. Two women went missing after sunbathing on a dock at the lake. Their bodies were found the next morning. At least one investigator felt an electrical shock on the dock during initial emergency response. The accident has been ruled an Electric Shock Drowning. Technical details will be updated when known.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Two women found dead following disappearance from dock at Lake Tuscaloosa. Ruled Electric Shock Drowning. At least one first responder felt electrical shock on dock. Detailed cause not yet reported at time of document revision.
