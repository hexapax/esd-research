## Description

Interviews with divers in Florida revealed many reports of high-level tingle while cleaning boat bottoms. All salt water. No fatalities.

## Classification Notes

Aggregate report spanning 2002-2003, not a single incident. Filed as NM-2002-001.md using 2002. Salt water — all incidents. Represents systemic issue with hull-cleaning divers.
