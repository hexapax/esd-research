## Description

Seven children behind a houseboat received electric shocks at Jamestown Resort and Marina on Lake Cumberland, Kentucky. No fatalities. A loss of neutral caused the issue.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Seven children shocked — all survived. Loss of neutral as cause.
