# NM-2014-001

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | April 27, 2014 |
| **Location** | Hialeah FL, condominium community pool |

| **Outcome** | Dead: 0, Injured: 0, Shocked/survived: 5 |
| **Source Section** | Near Misses |
| **PDF Item Number(s)** | NM #32 |

## Description

Three children and two adults were shocked from an improperly grounded pool pump at a condominium community pool in Hialeah, Florida. The children recovered after 4 days.

## Classification Notes

Non-marina -- community pool. Improperly grounded pump. 5 victims, all survived. Children needed 4 days recovery.
