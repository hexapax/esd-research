## Description

A dog was electrocuted at Paugus Bay, Laconia, New Hampshire. The owner felt a strong shock while wading in the water. A power pole with a flood light wire in the water was the cause.

## Classification Notes

Dog fatality, owner survived. Power pole/flood light as source. Outcome: Dead: 0 (human), Injured: 0, Shocked/survived: 1 (dog killed but not counted as human fatality).
