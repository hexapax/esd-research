## Description

Two boys with flotation devices were found face down behind a houseboat at Cedar Hill Lake, Smithville, Tennessee. The 7-year-old could not be revived.

## Classification Notes

Approximate date (month only). Flotation devices present -- shock paralysis despite flotation. One boy survived.
