## Description

Harris County, TX, hotel pool. A teenager was killed by electricity in a hotel pool that was not permitted to be open. Light fixture wires were exposed and the teen allegedly came in contact with them. The pool failed several inspections prior in an attempt to open.

## Classification Notes

Classified FI: Teenager killed by exposed light fixture wires in hotel pool. Pool was not permitted to be open and had failed several prior inspections. Direct contact electrocution (not pure ESD) but included in the ESD Drownings list.
