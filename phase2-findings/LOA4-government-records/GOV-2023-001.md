## Description

Thomas Shepard Milner jumped from his family's private dock on Lake Lanier on July 27, 2023, and was electrocuted upon entering the water. A family friend heard him scream. A neighbor entered a boat and jumped into the water to pull Milner out, immediately felt a burning electrical shock sensation, swam to shore, turned off the dock power box, and then returned to assist with the rescue. Milner was taken to Northside Forsyth Hospital and died the next day, July 28, 2023. The Forsyth County Sheriff's Office investigated.

## Cause

Stray electrical current from private dock infrastructure energizing the lake water. The neighbor's ability to cut the dock power box and then safely retrieve Milner confirms the current source was the dock electrical system — turning off the dock power ended the hazard.
