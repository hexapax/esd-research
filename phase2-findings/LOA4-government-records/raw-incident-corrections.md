## Additional Details

### FI-1989-001
- Was working on submerged water pump electrical installation
- Found with blood on rocks; body recovered from 25 feet of water

### FI-2011-002
- $50M wrongful death lawsuit: MI Court of Appeals COA 304695

### FI-2016-007
- Cause refined: missing ground and faulty dock light switch (ESDPA says "faulty lighting fixture")
- Location in Winston County confirmed

### FI-2017-012
- Both victims were University of Alabama School of Law staff
- Tuscaloosa Police identified faulty dock wiring sending current through water
- Deaths ruled accidental

### FI-2021-001
- KY Fire Marshal shut down Prospect Yacht Club after the death
- OSHA, USCG, and KY Labor all declined jurisdiction — regulatory gap documented
- ~9 month date discrepancy is the largest in the dataset

### FI-2024-001
- Stray voltage from electric boat lift on dock
- 2019–2020 attempt to modify Virginia building code for dock electrical safety failed due to industry opposition
