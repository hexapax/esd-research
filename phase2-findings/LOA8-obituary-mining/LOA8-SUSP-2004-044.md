## Data Discrepancy: Date of Incident

**Important:** The ESDPA incident list has an internal inconsistency on the date of this Lake Waccamaw incident:
- **2016 and 2021 ESDPA lists (including AuGres summary):** "June 19, 2004"
- **2024 ESDPA list:** "June 5, 2004"

The SUSP-2004-044 file uses "June 19, 2004" (matching the majority of ESDPA versions). However, the 2024 correction may represent an update to the original data. Additionally, the ESDPA list also has a separate entry for "June 5, 2004, Lake Wylie, Charlotte, NC" (two boys, houseboat, different incident) — it is possible that in the 2024 version, the Lake Waccamaw date was incorrectly updated to match the Lake Wylie date.

**Conclusion:** The true date is most likely June 19, 2004 based on consistency across multiple earlier ESDPA list versions.
