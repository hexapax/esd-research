# ESD Incident Report: NEW-2014-001

## Victim
- **Name:** Madeline "Maddy" Roskie
- **Age:** 18
- **Outcome:** Fatal

## Incident Details
- **Date:** August 2, 2014
- **Location:** Puyallup River near 96th Street Bridge, east of Tacoma, Washington
- **Body of Water Type:** Freshwater river
- **Electrical Source:** Ungrounded irrigation pump discharging 280V into the river via a pipe; power supplied by Puget Sound Energy
- **Circumstances:** Roskie was inner-tubing on the Puyallup River with her boyfriend. On their second run, she became unresponsive in the water and died. PSE technician found the source was an irrigation pump on an adjacent property with a "280v to ground issue." The pipe connected to the pump extended into the river. Family filed wrongful death lawsuit against PSE in 2017.

## Classification
- **Type:** Electric Shock Drowning (in-water electrocution from pump electrifying river water)
- **Verification Status:** VERIFIED (multiple independent sources)
