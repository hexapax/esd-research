# NEW-1999-002 — Lucas Ritz, 8 — Multnomah Channel, Portland OR (1999)

## Incident Summary

| **Location** | McCuddy's Marina, Multnomah Channel, Portland, Oregon |
| **Classification** | Fatal Incident |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 |

**Description:** Lucas Ritz, age 8, was swimming near a dock at McCuddy's Marina on the Multnomah Channel near Portland, Oregon. He was electrocuted by stray electrical current in the water caused by a fault in a boat's shore power connection. His death was initially classified as a drowning; it was only reclassified as an electrocution after his father Kevin Ritz (a marine technician) investigated the electrical conditions at the dock. Kevin Ritz subsequently co-founded the Electric Shock Drowning Prevention Association (ESDPA) with Elise Lutrick (mother of Ken Lutrick, FI-2004-003).
