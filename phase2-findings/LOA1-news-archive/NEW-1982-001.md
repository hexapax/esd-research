# NEW-1982-001: G. Brockett Irwin — Lake O' The Pines, TX (Dec 1982)

## Status
UNVERIFIED LEAD — single secondary source only

## Basic Info
- **Date:** December 1982 (exact date unknown)
- **Location:** Lake O' The Pines, near Johnson Creek, East Texas (Marion/Upshur County area)
- **Victim:** G. Brockett Irwin (attorney) — Fatal
- **Outcome:** Fatal (victim); 3 other occupants survived
- **Other victims:** 3 unnamed occupants, survived

## ESD Mechanism
Irwin was aboard a sailboat whose mast struck an overhead power line crossing Lake O' The Pines near Johnson Creek. The incident is nearly identical in mechanism to the 2017 Boy Scout incident at the same lake. After this incident, Upshur Rural Electric Cooperative reportedly raised the power line and installed danger buoys in the area, but the clearance was apparently not extended across the full waterway.

**ESD classification note:** Power line strike from boat mast — ESD-ADJACENT mechanism, not traditional dock/marina/shore power ESD. However, the resulting energization of the water/boat caused the victim's death.

## Notes
- Tom Irwin (brother of victim) is quoted in the 2017 article: "a very similar event occurred on this very same lake 35 years ago... In December of 1982, attorney G. Brockett Irwin was electrocuted after his sailboat's mast struck a power line over Lake O' The Pines near Johnson Creek."
- This incident establishes that the utility knew of the power-line-over-water hazard at this location 35 years before the 2017 Boy Scout deaths.
- If verified, this would be one of the earliest documented lake power-line electrocution incidents in Texas.
