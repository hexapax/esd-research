## Incident Summary (for index purposes)
- **Date:** August 10, 2017
- **Location:** Columbia River at Pasco, Franklin County, Washington State
- **Victim:** Cole Grad, age 15, male
- **Outcome:** Fatal — drowned after electrical shock
- **Type:** Electric Shock Drowning — contact with electrified irrigation pump pipe while in river water
- **Verification Status:** VERIFIED (5+ independent sources)
- **State:** Washington

## Description
Cole Grad, a 15-year-old from Pasco, WA, was swimming in the Columbia River near a private pump house. He touched an electrified metal pipe connected to a faulty irrigation pump while in or entering the water. Two Pasco firefighters were also shocked during initial rescue. The Franklin County Coroner ruled "accidental drowning upon low voltage electrical shock." Washington State L&I confirmed faulty pump wiring.
