# NEW-UNKNOWN-001: Two Juvenile Females — Lake Travis, TX (Dock ESD)

## Incident Summary
- **Victims:** Two juvenile females (names unknown)
- **Date:** Unknown (referenced in training materials, likely pre-2010)
- **Location:** Lake Travis, Texas
- **Type:** Electric shock drowning (dock/houseboat electrical fault)
- **Outcome:** Fatal (presumed, based on description)

## Description
Two juvenile females jumped into the water off a dock at Lake Travis and were electrocuted. Witnesses described them as having "eyes wide open, mouth wide open, very stiff and unable to talk" before going limp and rolling over in the water. A detective's report noted: "I believe that this other juvenile witnessed the electric shock until the breaker was tripped," though the breaker most likely did not trip. The cause was determined to be a lack of grounding system and a faulty battery charger on a houseboat.

## Cause
Lack of grounding system and faulty battery charger on a houseboat at the dock.
