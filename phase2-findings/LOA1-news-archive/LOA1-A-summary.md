## Analysis

The gap years likely represent genuine gaps in the documented record rather than missed incidents:

1. **1990** falls between FI-1989-001 (Dec 1989) and FI-1991-001 (corrected to July 1991). The CDC MMWR report covering Oklahoma 1989-1993 explicitly has no 1990 case.
2. **1992** falls between FI-1991-002 (July 1991, Lake Hamilton AR) and FI-1993-001 (1993). The CDC MMWR Oklahoma cluster also has no 1992 case.
3. **1996** falls between FI-1995-001 and FI-1997-001. No national surveillance existed at that time.

The absence of incidents likely reflects:
- Small annual incidence (~3-5 ESD deaths/year nationally in this era)
- No national surveillance or reporting mechanism existed before ~2000
- Random year-to-year variation in a rare event
- Some 1990/1992/1996 incidents may have occurred but were classified as simple drownings with no electrical investigation
