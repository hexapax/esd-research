# NEW-2023-002: Dr. Marshall L. Meadors III — Lake Hartwell, SC

## Incident Summary
- **Date:** July 17, 2023
- **Location:** Lake Hartwell, Mid Lake Heights Drive, Anderson County, South Carolina
- **Victim:** Dr. Marshall L. Meadors III, age 65, of Anderson, SC
- **Outcome:** Fatal (pronounced dead at hospital)
- **Type:** Electric Shock Drowning — dock electrocution (exposed wires)

## Description
Dr. Meadors was swimming at Lake Hartwell near Mid Lake Heights Drive when he went missing Monday evening. Authorities were called around 8 p.m. Investigators found the victim in the water and transported him to a hospital where he was pronounced dead. The investigation revealed exposed electrical wires on an electrical box providing power to the dock. The Anderson County Coroner ruled the preliminary cause of death as "freshwater drowning possibly secondary to electrocution." An autopsy was scheduled.

## Classification
- **ESD Confirmed:** Probable — exposed dock wiring found; coroner cited electrocution as possible contributing factor
- **Verification:** VERIFIED (3+ independent sources)
