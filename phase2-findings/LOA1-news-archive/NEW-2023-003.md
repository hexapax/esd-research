# NEW-2023-003: Dr. Kole Nikprelaj — Wright Island Marina, New Rochelle, NY

## Incident Summary
- **Date:** July 9, 2023
- **Location:** Wright Island Marina (Drake Ave. Marina, Inc.), New Rochelle, Westchester County, New York
- **Victim:** Dr. Kole Shtjefni Nikprelaj, age 24, of Bedford, NY
- **Second Victim:** Dila Bujaj (girlfriend) — shocked, survived
- **Outcome:** Fatal for Nikprelaj (removed from life support July 30, 2023); non-fatal injury for Bujaj
- **Type:** Electric Shock Drowning / Electrocution — electrified boat ramp / live wire at marina

## Description
Nikprelaj and his girlfriend Dila Bujaj had just finished jet-skiing at Wright Island Marina when both were electrocuted by a live wire in the water near the dock/boat ramp. Both were found unconscious in the water by a bystander at the marina. Both were pulled from the water and transported to a local hospital. Bujaj survived; Nikprelaj was placed on life support and died on July 30, 2023, approximately three weeks after the incident.

Fire officials stated the boat ramp had become electrified. A wrongful death lawsuit was filed on November 11, 2024 (Lisa Nikpreljaj v. ELQ Industries, Inc., Verde Electric Corp., M F Electrical Service Co., Inc., and Drake Ave. Marina, Inc. d/b/a Wright Island Marina) in Westchester County Supreme Court.
