## New Verified Incidents Found: 4

### 1. NEW-2023-001 — Thomas Shepard Milner
- **Date:** July 27, 2023
- **Location:** Lake Lanier, Forsyth County, Georgia
- **Victim:** Thomas Shepard Milner, 24
- **Outcome:** Fatal

### 2. NEW-2023-002 — Dr. Marshall L. Meadors III

## Notable Observations
1. All 4 new incidents occurred in 2017 or 2023 — none from the 1980s–2010s search window, suggesting either under-documentation in earlier decades or genuine rarity.
2. Three of the four incidents involved victims age 24 or younger.
3. The July 2023 cluster (three incidents in 22 days across GA, SC, and NY) is striking.
4. South Carolina introduced Bill 3070 (2021-2022 session) requiring commercial dock/marina electrical safety inspections, suggesting legislative awareness of the risk.
5. The Chesapeake Bay's predominantly saltwater environment may reduce ESD risk compared to freshwater lakes, consistent with the absence of incidents in that region.
