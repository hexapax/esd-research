# NEW-2020-001: Khaleel Marcos Reynolds — Houston, TX (Pool Electrocution)

## Incident Summary
- **Victim:** Khaleel Marcos Reynolds, age 15
- **Date:** August 29, 2020
- **Location:** North Villa Inn motel pool, 16510 North Freeway, Houston, TX (Harris County)
- **Type:** Pool electrocution (exposed pool light wiring)
- **Outcome:** Fatal

## Description
Khaleel Reynolds was swimming in the motel pool when he came into contact with exposed wires from an interior pool light that had been removed, leaving wires exposed. He was electrocuted and pulled from the pool by his father. Paramedics performed CPR and transported him to the hospital, where he died.

## Cause
An interior pool light had been removed, leaving electrical wires exposed in the pool. Harris Health inspection records showed the pool had failed inspections going back to 2018, including for exposed wiring violations. The pool did not have a valid operating permit at the time of the incident.
