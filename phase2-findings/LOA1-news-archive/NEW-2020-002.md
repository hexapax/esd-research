# NEW-2020-002: Timothy Miller & Michael Miller — Lake Pleasant, AZ (Marina ESD)

## Incident Summary
- **Victims:** Timothy Miller, age 53; Michael Miller, age 50 (brothers)
- **Date:** July 12, 2020 (incident); bodies recovered July 13, 2020
- **Location:** Scorpion Bay Marina, Lake Pleasant, Peoria, AZ (Maricopa County)
- **Type:** Marina/dock electric shock drowning (boat electrical fault)
- **Outcome:** Fatal (2 deaths), 1 additional injury (Timothy's girlfriend — burn marks on feet/legs)

## Description
The Miller brothers and their wives were at their docked boat at Scorpion Bay Marina around 6:30 PM. Timothy jumped into the water near the boat's swim platform and immediately experienced electrical current. Michael jumped in to rescue his brother and also became incapacitated. Timothy's girlfriend also jumped in and experienced shocks. Bystanders pulled Michael and the girlfriend from the water. Michael was transported to a hospital where he was pronounced dead. Timothy's body was found by a dive team under a dock in the early morning hours of July 13.

## Cause
MCSO investigation determined the marina's electrical infrastructure was operating properly and safely. The brothers' boat had an electrical connection system that was incompatible with the marina's receptacle. Modifications to the boat's electrical system had bypassed safety features, resulting in electricity being discharged into the water around the swim platform.
