# NEW-2023-001: Thomas Shepard Milner — Lake Lanier, GA

## Incident Summary
- **Date:** July 27, 2023
- **Location:** Lake Lanier, near Dove Trail / Lanier Beach South Road, Forsyth County, Georgia
- **Victim:** Thomas Shepard Milner, age 24, of Forsyth County, GA
- **Outcome:** Fatal (died July 28, 2023 at Northside Forsyth Hospital)
- **Type:** Electric Shock Drowning — dock electrocution

## Description
Milner jumped into the lake from his family's private dock and was heard screaming for help shortly after entering the water. A neighbor took a boat over and one person jumped in to help but felt a burning sensation recognized as electrical shock. That rescuer swam ashore, turned off a power box near the dock, then re-entered the water to pull Milner out. CPR was administered by Milner's uncle until medics arrived, but Milner died the next day at the hospital.

The dock was less than three years old and had been outfitted with electricity by a licensed electrician, according to the victim's mother.

## Classification
- **ESD Confirmed:** Yes — electrical current from dock energized the water
- **Verification:** VERIFIED (5+ independent sources)
