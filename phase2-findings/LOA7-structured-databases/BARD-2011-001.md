## BARD Narrative

Based on witness statements two individuals were swimming behind a houseboat on Norris Lake. The houseboat had been experiencing some electrical issues, namely a breaker under the sink that was tripping on a regular basis. While the two individuals were in the water the breaker tripped and was reset by an individual onboard the vessel. When the breaker was reset the individuals in the water began to feel an electric shock. One individual had to be pulled from the water as a result of some degree of paralysis.

## Analysis

Classic houseboat ESD near-miss: faulty electrical system leaking current into surrounding water. The breaker tripping was itself a safety mechanism responding to current leakage; resetting it re-energized the water. The 9-year-old victim experienced paralysis — a hallmark of ESD.
