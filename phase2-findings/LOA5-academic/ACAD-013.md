## Abstract/Summary
Short awareness article in the IEEE Industry Applications Magazine — the flagship publication of the IEEE Industry Applications Society — raising awareness about electric shock drowning among electrical engineers and industry professionals. Published as volume 26, issue 1, pages 5-6, 68, January/February 2020.

The IEEE Industry Applications Society membership includes electrical engineers working in power systems, marina electrical systems, and industrial facilities — precisely the professionals involved in designing and maintaining the electrical infrastructure that can cause ESD.

## Notes
The publication of an ESD awareness piece in the IEEE's Industry Applications Magazine is significant because it indicates that by 2020, ESD had reached sufficient prominence that the main professional society for electrical engineers felt it warranted coverage. The IEEE IAS is extremely influential in setting standards and practices for electrical engineering, so this article would have reached a very large professional audience. The appearance of this article alongside the Morse et al. (2020) IEEE Transactions paper (ACAD-007) suggests a broader push within IEEE to address ESD from an engineering standards perspective in that period. A discussion paper by Vesa Linja-aho in IEEE Transactions on Industry Applications Vol. 57, No. 1 (Jan/Feb 2021) followed the Morse et al. paper.
