## Code Legend

**Drowning (W-codes):**
- W65: Bathtub
- W67: Swimming pool
- W68: Pool in water
- W69: Natural water / open water ← ESD-relevant
- W70: Natural running water ← ESD-relevant
- W73: Other specified ← ESD-relevant
- W74: Unspecified ← ESD-relevant

**Electrical (W-codes):**
- W85: Power/transmission lines
- W86: Other specified electrical (dock/marina wiring) ← highest ESD specificity
- W87: Unspecified electrical

**Effects (T-codes, appear on almost all records):**
- T751: Effects of drowning/submersion
- T754: Effects of electric current

**Other notable codes in this dataset:**
- X45/X44: Accidental alcohol poisoning
- T510/T519: Toxic effects of alcohol
- T149: Injury of unspecified body region
- I119: Hypertensive heart disease with renal failure
- I250/I251: Coronary artery disease
- I469/I499: Cardiac arrest
- I500: Heart failure
- R55: Syncope
- R568: Other seizures
- X37: Cataclysm/natural disaster (with 2023 W86 record — unusual)
- T750: Effects of lightning
