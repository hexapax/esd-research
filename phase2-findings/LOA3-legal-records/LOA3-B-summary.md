## Key Legal Findings
1. **Governmental immunity** is a major barrier: Both the Knudsen (MI) and Anderson (MO) cases were dismissed on immunity grounds despite clear ESD causation
2. **Recreational Use Acts** shield utilities/landowners: Missouri Supreme Court ruled 5-2 that Ameren was immune despite charging dock permit fees
3. **Known verdicts range from $150K to $6M**: Crays ($150K jury), Harbison ($2.325M jury), lifeguard ($6M settlement)
4. **Many settlements are confidential**: Davenport and Hernandez cases settled but amounts undisclosed
5. **NEC Article 555.24** (marina warning signs) is increasingly cited in complaints (Marina Shores case)
6. **Product liability** (Pentair pool lights, pool pumps) is a distinct litigation track from marina/dock ESD
