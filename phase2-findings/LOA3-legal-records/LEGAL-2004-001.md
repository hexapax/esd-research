# LEGAL-2004-001: Chipley v. Scott Creek Marina — Cave Run Lake, KY

## Incident Summary
- **Victim:** Samantha Chipley, age 19 (died)
- **Date:** September 2004
- **Location:** Scott's Creek Marina, Cave Run Lake, Morehead, Rowan County, Kentucky
- **Type:** Electric shock drowning — submerged battery charger on houseboat energized hull; no bonding system; victim entered water and was electrocuted
- **Existing Dataset ID:** FI-2004-001

## Legal Details
- **Case Type:** Wrongful death
- **Outcome:** **$700,000 settlement** — reached at a court-ordered settlement conference one week before trial
- **Details:** The settlement resolved claims related to the marina's failure to maintain proper electrical bonding on houseboats and the absence of GFCI protection. The submerged battery charger on a neighboring houseboat energized the hull, leaking current into the surrounding water.
