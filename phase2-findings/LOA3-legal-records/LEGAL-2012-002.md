# LEGAL-2012-002: Jojo v. Orange Lake Country Club — Orange County, FL

## Incident Summary
- **Victim:** Ashton Jojo, age 11 (died)
- **Date:** June 2012
- **Location:** Orange Lake Resorts mini-golf course pond, Orange County, Florida
- **Type:** Electrocution in water — girl retrieved golf ball from pond; electrocuted by improperly installed electrical wiring/pumps

## Legal Details
- **Plaintiff:** Jojo family
- **Defendant:** Orange Lake Country Club / Orange Lake Resorts
- **Law Firm:** Leesfield & Partners (plaintiff)
- **Case Type:** Wrongful death
- **Damages Sought:** $27 million (medical expenses, mental pain, loss of companionship, funeral costs)
- **Allegations:** Pumps installed without proper permits; work done by unlicensed electricians; improper grounding
- **Outcome:** Not publicly reported
