## ESD Mechanism
Lankford was swimming with her two stepbrothers (ages 11 and 13) near a private dock when the group felt a current in the water. The stepbrothers swam to a separate dock and were unharmed. Lankford swam toward the dock where current was present and was electrocuted when she touched something metallic on it. Sgt. Paul Reinsch of Missouri State Highway Patrol confirmed she "was electrocuted when she touched a dock that apparently had faulty wiring." Several electrical problems were found on the dock. She could not be revived at the scene. This was the third ESD death at Lake of the Ozarks within a single week in July 2012 (following the Anderson children on July 4).
