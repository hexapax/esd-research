# LEGAL-2025-001: Gonzalez v. Marina Shores — Portage, IN

## Incident Summary
- **Victims:** Gabriel Gonzalez (died); Francis Gonzalez III (brother, seriously injured/paralyzed during rescue attempt)
- **Date:** July 10, 2025
- **Location:** Marina Shores, Portage, Indiana
- **Type:** ESD — Gabriel jumped into water to retrieve dropped item while cleaning boat; shocked and drowned; Francis shocked attempting rescue. Similar incident had occurred 4 days prior.

## Legal Details
- **Plaintiff:** Gonzalez family
- **Defendant:** Marina Shores owners/operators
- **Law Firm:** Schafer & Schafer LLP (Todd Schafer)
- **Case Type:** Wrongful death and personal injury
- **Allegations:** Failure to post NEC Article 555.24 warning signs; failure to address known electrical hazards; failure to maintain electrical systems; prior similar incident 4 days before
- **Damages Sought:** Compensatory and punitive damages (amount unspecified)
- **Outcome:** Pending (as of 2025)
