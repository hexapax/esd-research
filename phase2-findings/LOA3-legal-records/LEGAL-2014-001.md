# LEGAL-2014-001: Orvis v. Silverleaf Resorts — Piney Shores Resort, Lake Conroe, TX

## Incident Summary
- **Victim:** Andrew Orvis, age 8 (died)
- **Date:** July 20, 2014 (some sources say July 10, 2014)
- **Location:** Piney Shores Resort dock, Lake Conroe, Montgomery County, Texas
- **Type:** Dock electrocution — boy sat on dock, dangled feet in water; exposed live wiring on dock caused electrocution; fell into water and drowned

## Legal Details
- **Plaintiffs:** Mother and father of Andrew Orvis (filed separate lawsuits)
- **Defendant:** Silverleaf Resorts, Inc. (Dallas-based, operator of Piney Shores Resort)
- **Attorney:** Randall Sorrels (Houston)
- **Court:** Montgomery County District Court, Texas
- **Case Type:** Wrongful death, negligence
- **Allegations:** Silverleaf negligently maintained dock with exposed live wiring; failed to prevent access; failed to warn; malfunctioning safety mechanisms; city inspectors found exposed live wiring 2 days after death
- **Damages Sought:** More than $1 million
- **Outcome:** Not publicly reported
