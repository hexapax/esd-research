## ESD Mechanism
Thomas Milner jumped off his family's dock at Lake Lanier from a dock equipped with an electrical power box supplying electricity to a boat lift. He began screaming for help immediately after entering the water. A family friend attempted rescue with a ladder; neighbors arrived by boat and one person jumped into the water to pull Milner out but felt "a burning sensation he recognized as electrical shock." That neighbor swam ashore, turned off the power box adjacent to the dock, and then returned to pull Milner out. He was transported to Northside Forsyth Hospital where he died the next day.

The dock was less than three years old and had been wired by a licensed electrician, per the victim's mother. Investigation by authorities was ongoing as of the last available reports. The power box for the boat lift is the probable source of current leakage into the water.
