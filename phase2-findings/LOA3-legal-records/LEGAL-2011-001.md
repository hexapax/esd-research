# LEGAL-2011-001: Knudsen v. City of Traverse City — Clinch Park Marina, MI

## Incident Summary
- **Victim:** Michael Scott Knudsen, age 18 (died); Zachary Kott-Millard (injured rescuer)
- **Date:** August 15, 2011
- **Location:** Clinch Park Marina (F-Dock), Traverse City, Michigan
- **Type:** ESD — 18-year-old drowned from electric shock while swimming near marina dock; faulty electrical wiring

## Legal Details
- **Plaintiff:** Estate of Michael Scott Knudsen; Zachary Kott-Millard
- **Defendant:** City of Traverse City; marina officials Robert Cole, Barry Smith; SmithGroup JJR LLC; Rhoades Engineering
- **Law Firm(s):** Geoffrey Fieger; Dean Robb; Jeffrey Danzig (plaintiff attorneys)
- **Case Type:** $50 million wrongful death lawsuit
- **Outcome:** Partially dismissed in 2013 — judge ruled marina operation was governmental function; city granted governmental immunity; no gross negligence finding against marina director. Case proceeded against other defendants but no final verdict/settlement reported.
- **Related:** Legal malpractice suits against attorney Geoffrey Fieger also dismissed
