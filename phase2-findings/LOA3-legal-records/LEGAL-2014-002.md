## Incident Summary
- **Victim:** Calder Sloan, age 7 (died)

- **Location:** Family swimming pool, North Miami, Miami-Dade County, Florida
- **Type:** Pool electrocution — faulty pool light manufactured by Pentair; failures in grounding, bonding, and maintenance
