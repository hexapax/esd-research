# LEGAL-2006-001: Harbison v. AmerenUE — Spring Lake, Jefferson County, MO

## Incident Summary
- **Victim:** Nicholas "Nic" Harbison, age 16 (died); Morgan Milfeld, 15; Timothy Fitzpatrick, 15; Joshua McClure, 18 (injured/resuscitated)
- **Date:** March 18, 2006
- **Location:** Spring Lake, Jefferson County, Missouri
- **Type:** ESD — teens jumped from dock into lake; immobilized by stray voltage from AmerenUE underground power cable with corroded neutrals

## Legal Details
- **Plaintiff:** Jerry and Tina Harbison (wrongful death); Milfeld and Fitzpatrick families (personal injury)
- **Defendant:** AmerenUE (Union Electric)
- **Law Firm(s):** Not specified in sources
- **Case Type:** Wrongful death and personal injury
- **Outcome:** Jury verdict (9-3) against AmerenUE
  - $1,250,000 to Harbison family
  - $725,000 to Fitzpatrick family
  - $350,000 to Milfeld family
  - **Total: $2,325,000**
