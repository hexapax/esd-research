# LEGAL-2010-001: Crays v. Angel Shores Mobile Home Park — Tippecanoe County, IN

## Incident Summary
- **Victim:** Zachary Crays, age 13 (died)
- **Date:** July 25, 2010
- **Location:** Angel Shores Mobile Home Park, Tippecanoe County, Indiana (swimming at home of Bryan and Donna Wagner, lessees)
- **Type:** ESD — 13-year-old electrocuted while swimming; electrical source from property/dock infrastructure

## Legal Details
- **Plaintiff:** John Crays and Megan Crays (parents)
- **Defendant:** Angel Shores Mobile Home Park, Inc.; Bryan and Donna Wagner
- **Law Firm(s):** Amicus: Robert W. Johnson, Johnson Jensen LLP, Indianapolis
- **Case Filed:** January 28, 2011
- **Case Number:** Court of Appeals Case No. 79A02-1605-CT-1106
- **Outcome:** Jury verdict of $150,000; plus $60,000 attorney fees and $72,864.85 litigation costs = **$282,864.85 total judgment**
  - Reduced by $200,000 advance payment made prior to trial
  - Angel Shores appealed attorney fees award; appeal decided June 20, 2017
- **Citation:** Angel Shores Mobile Home Park, Inc. v. Crays, 78 N.E.3d 718 (Ind. Ct. App. 2017)
