## Interpretation Notes

1. **W69+W86 is the "gold standard" ESD code pair**: Open water drowning + other specified electrical current. Found 5 times in the dataset. These records most directly represent dock/marina ESD.

2. **W74 (unspecified drowning) dominance**: 15 of 42 candidates (36%) use unspecified drowning. This is consistent with cases where the exact circumstances were uncertain or where ESD was suspected but the water type was not specified in the record.

3. **T751+T754 universal signature**: Nearly all records contain both the drowning effects code (T751) and the electrical effects code (T754). This confirms dual-mechanism recognition by the certifying physician/ME.

4. **Alcohol co-occurrence**: Records 20, 23, 26, 36, 40 include alcohol codes (X44/X45, T510/T519). This suggests alcohol may have been a contributing factor in these ESD deaths — consistent with recreational dock/marina settings where alcohol consumption is common.

5. **Cardiac comorbidity**: Records 18, 31, 34, 41, and 37 include cardiac codes (I25x, I119, I469/I500). Pre-existing heart disease may have made victims more susceptible to fatal ESD at lower current levels.

6. **2023 Record 38 (X37)**: The combination of W69+W86+X37 (cataclysm/natural disaster) in 2023 is unusual — may represent flooding or storm-damaged electrical infrastructure.
