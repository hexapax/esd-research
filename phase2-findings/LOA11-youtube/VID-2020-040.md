# VID-2020-040: Khaleel Marcos Reynolds — Texas Hotel Pool Electrocution

## Incident Summary
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | August 29, 2020 |
| **Location** | North Houston, Harris County, Texas (North Villa Inn, 16510 North Freeway) |
| **Classification** | Fatal electrocution (pool) |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 (Khaleel Marcos Reynolds, age 15), Injured: 0 |

**Description:** Fifteen-year-old Khaleel Marcos Reynolds was swimming in the outdoor pool at the North Villa Inn & Banquet Hall when he touched an exposed wire from an interior pool light that had been removed from its housing. He was electrocuted and died at hospital. The pool had never been permitted to operate and had failed county inspections in July 2018 and December 2018. Harris County Health Department found the pool still in violation the day after Reynolds' death. The boy's father pulled him from the water and performed CPR. His mother sued the motel owner for wrongful death. Two electricians-equivalent citations were raised. The pool incident falls under ESD-adjacent category (pool wiring electrocution, not open-water marina ESD, but same failure mechanism).
