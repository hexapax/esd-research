## Gaps Identified — Incidents Without YouTube Documentation Found

The following incidents were searched but no YouTube video documentation was located:

1. **Lake of the Ozarks deaths 2016-2017** — Angela Anderson stated "four people dead at the Lake of the Ozarks since 2015" (March 2017 testimony). One is Marcus Colburn (2015). Three more deaths between 2015 and March 2017 remain unidentified by name in this search. KOLR (Springfield/Joplin CBS) would be the station most likely to have covered these.

2. **Arkansas Lake Hamilton incidents** — KATV (Little Rock) has reported "5 ESD deaths in Arkansas" but specific incidents beyond the 2008 and 1991 and 2001 ESDPA-listed ones (Lake Hamilton, Bull Shoals) were not found in YouTube searches. The 2024 KATV story about a body recovered from Lake Hamilton was a non-ESD drowning.

3. **Florida, Oklahoma, Kentucky, West Virginia, Michigan** — General searches returned national/awareness content but no incident-specific videos from these states in this session. Michigan's Clinch Marina/Park incident was not found via YouTube searches.
