**Description:** Dr. Eric Hughes, a 37-year-old orthodontist from Tuscaloosa, died in August 2015 after going swimming in the lake after cutting his grass one afternoon. His friends found him in about four feet of water near his pontoon boat. Investigators believe he was shocked by electricity before he drowned. His official cause of death was ruled as drowning, not electrocution. However, Tuscaloosa County Homicide Capt. Kip Hart stated at the 2017 press conference about the Darling/Whipple deaths: "we do believe that electrical current played a part in that."
