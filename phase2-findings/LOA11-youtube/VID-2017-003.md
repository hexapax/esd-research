# VID-2017-003: Evan Currie — YouTube Discovery

## Incident Summary
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | June 16, 2017 |
| **Location** | Miller Marina, Put-in-Bay, Ottawa County, Ohio (Lake Erie) |
| **Classification** | Fatal ESD — marina shore power |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 (Evan Currie, age 19, of Dublin, Ohio), Injured: 1 (Jeffrey Currie, Evan's father — shocked, survived); 1 brother also shocked, survived |

**Description:** The Currie family of Dublin, Ohio docked their 33-foot power boat at Miller Marina, Put-in-Bay. After plugging into shore power, their dog Daisy fell into the electrically-charged water. Father Jeffrey Currie jumped in to rescue the dog, immediately feeling a strong electric shock like "being hit with a stun gun." Evan and his younger brother jumped in to help their father. Evan managed to shout "electricity" between convulsions. At the urging of bystanders, Mrs. Currie unplugged the boat's shore power. After the brothers and father returned to the boat, Evan was unresponsive. He was administered CPR and taken to Port Clinton but died at the scene. An investigation by an electrician found all power outlets nearby were functioning properly at the time. The exact source of the electrical fault was unclear.
