| **Outcome** | Dead: 0, Injured: 3 children (all survived) — 2 girls paralyzed by shock; grandfather and father also shocked trying to rescue |

**Description:** CCTV footage captured two children suffering electric shocks after touching a metal handrail in a swimming pool at a Florida apartment complex. One girl's body suddenly froze after she touched the metal handrail; her father sitting on the steps pulled her to safety while also getting shocked. As other children fled the pool, a second girl remained paralyzed with her hand stuck to the railing. Her grandfather ran into the pool and dragged her out, then fell back on the pool deck from the electric current. A 10-year-old boy was also reportedly shocked. All three children survived. Police blamed unconnected ground wires in the pool's pump house.
