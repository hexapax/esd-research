# VID-2013-001: Daniel Petersen — YouTube Discovery

## Incident Summary
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | July 4, 2013 (died July 7, 2013) |
| **Location** | Eagle Lake (Big Eagle Lake), Orrock Township, Sherburne County, Minnesota |
| **Classification** | Fatal ESD |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 (Daniel Petersen, age 46), Injured: 1 (Kris Biser, his 49-year-old sister — survived) |

**Description:** Daniel Petersen of Big Lake, MN was attempting to charge his boat battery while standing in about 20 inches of water at the shore of Eagle Lake. He was holding a plugged-in battery charger and lost his footing, falling into the water while the charger was still connected to an electrical socket. He was electrocuted. His sister Kris Biser ran into the water to help and was also shocked and rendered unconscious. Neighbors pulled the electrical cord from the socket and pulled both from the water. Daniel was airlifted to Hennepin County Medical Center and died three days later on July 7. Kris was transported to St. Cloud Hospital and survived.
