# VID-2023-001: Thomas "Shepard" Milner — YouTube Discovery

## Incident Summary
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | July 27, 2023 (died July 28, 2023) |
| **Location** | Lake Lanier, near Lanier Beach South Road / Dove Trail, Forsyth County, Georgia |
| **Classification** | Fatal ESD — private dock electrocution |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 (Thomas Shepard "Shep" Milner, age 24), Injured: 1 neighbor (felt burning sensation in water, exited safely) |

**Description:** Thomas Shepard Milner (24) jumped off his family's dock into Lake Lanier near Dove Trail in Forsyth County. He was heard screaming for help shortly after entering the water. A family friend tried to get him out using a ladder but was unsuccessful. Neighbors took a boat to Milner and one person jumped in to pull him out but immediately felt "a burning sensation that he recognized as an electric shock." That neighbor swam ashore and turned off the power box supplying the dock's boat lift, then re-entered the water to pull Milner out. Milner's uncle administered CPR until first responders arrived. Milner was transported to Northside Forsyth Hospital where he died the next day. Georgia DNR reported that electricity came from a nearby power box. His mother Martha Milner said the dock was less than 3 years old and had been outfitted with electricity by a licensed electrician.
