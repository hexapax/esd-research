# VID-2016-001: Carmen Johnson — YouTube Discovery

## Incident Summary
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | April 16, 2016 |
| **Location** | Smith Lake (Lewis Smith Lake), Winston County, Alabama (family lake house) |
| **Classification** | Fatal ESD |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 (Carmen Johnson, age 15), Injured: 3 (Jimmy Johnson/father — shocked; Zach Johnson/brother — shocked; Reagan Gargis/friend — shocked, survived) |

**Description:** Carmen Johnson (15), a Priceville High School cheerleader, jumped off the family boat dock into Smith Lake with her friend Reagan Gargis. Her father Jimmy lowered a metal ladder into the water for the girls to climb out. A water-filled light switch on the dock sent electrical current through the ladder into the water. Both Carmen and Reagan were paralyzed. Jimmy and his son Zach jumped in to help and were also shocked. Casey (Jimmy's wife) ran to cut power at the dock switch. Reagan, Jimmy and Zach survived; Carmen sank too deep before power was cut and could not be retrieved. Carmen's parents have since become major ESD awareness advocates.
