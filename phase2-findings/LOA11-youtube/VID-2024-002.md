**Description:** Dianne Guyton was swimming in Smith Lake, Alabama approximately 2015 when she was electrocuted. She was swimming and reached up to the dock to get her phone when the electricity in the water electrocuted her. Her neighbors were able to bring her to shore. She survived but nearly drowned. As of June 2024, she was sharing her story to help warn others about ESD risks ahead of summer.
