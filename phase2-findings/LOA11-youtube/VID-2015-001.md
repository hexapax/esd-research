# VID-2015-001: Marcus Colburn — YouTube Discovery

| Field | Value |

| **Classification** | Fatal ESD |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 (Marcus Colburn, age 21, of East Moline, Illinois), Injured: 1 (Taylor Curley — felt electricity, survived) |

**Description:** Marcus Colburn (21) of East Moline, IL was swimming with Taylor Curley in Woods Hollow Cove at the Lake of the Ozarks. While swimming, Curley felt electricity in the water. Colburn tried to exit via the dock ladder but was shocked when he grabbed it and fell face-down into the lake. He was pulled from the water but could not be revived. Investigation by the Osage Beach Fire Department, Ameren Missouri, and Missouri Highway Patrol found a faulty junction box on the dock. Occupants had noticed the breaker was tripping; on the evening of the incident they attempted to reset it again. The breaker box had passed two inspections the prior year because only the exterior was checked.
