# VID-2024-001: Jesse Hamric — YouTube Discovery

## Incident Summary
| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Date** | July 4, 2024 (early morning, ~3:44 a.m.) |
| **Location** | Smith Mountain Lake, Lee Drive / Lead Drive, Huddleston, Bedford County, Virginia |
| **Classification** | Fatal ESD — private dock / boat lift |
| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 (Jesse Hamric, age 18, of Colorado), Injured: 2 friends (minor electric shocks trying to save him) |

**Description:** Jesse Hamric (18), visiting Smith Mountain Lake from Colorado with his family and friends for the July 4th holiday, dove into the lake from a private boat dock at approximately 3:44 a.m. He immediately became unresponsive due to stray voltage from an electric boat lift at the dock. Two of his friends jumped in to help and sustained minor electric shocks. They were able to pull Hamric from the water and began CPR. Smith Mountain Lake Marine Fire and Rescue Chief Todd Ohlerich stated the department's shock alert tester confirmed the presence of electric currents in the water. Hamric was transported to a local hospital where he was pronounced dead. The dock was at a private residence and the homeowner was notified. No foul play was suspected. Bedford County Sheriff's Office Major Jon Wilks confirmed the medical examiner's investigation was ongoing as of August 2024.
