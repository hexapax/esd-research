| **Outcome** | Dead: 1 (James DeAngelo, age 23, of Venetia, PA), Injured: unknown number of others shocked |

**Description:** James DeAngelo (23) of Venetia, PA was a visitor on a houseboat at Beach Club Marina on the Monongahela River on the Fourth of July. He jumped into the water for a swim. Eyewitness accounts at the scene prompted police to investigate electric shock drowning as a potential cause. Multiple other people in the water at the same time reportedly were also shocked. Monongahela police Chief Kevin Harris stated: "Numerous reports when we got down here, what individuals saw that night." The U.S. Coast Guard Auxiliary issued a statement following DeAngelo's death alerting the public to ESD risk. DeAngelo was a hockey player for California University of Pennsylvania (Vulcans Hockey Club) and from Franklin Regional. A hockey tribute was held for him in October 2023.
