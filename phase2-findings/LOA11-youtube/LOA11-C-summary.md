## Key Findings by Region

### Texas (SEVERE gap confirmed, but incidents found)
- **Three Texas fatalities identified**: Andrew Orvis (Lake Conroe, 2014), Raul Hernandez (Houston Hilton, 2013), Khaleel Reynolds (North Villa Inn, 2020)
- All three in the greater Houston area — suggests Houston's warm climate and dense water recreation creates concentration of risk
- No open-lake ESD incidents found in Texas interior lakes (Lake Travis, Lake Conroe marina incidents, Lake Texoma) — gap likely reflects underreporting
- The Raul Hernandez Hilton case is potentially ABSENT from ESDPA marina list (it's a pool); criminal charges and civil settlement make it highly verifiable
- KPRC 2, KHOU 11, and ABC13/KTRK all have YouTube coverage

### Arizona (First fatal incidents identified)
- **Double fatality** at Lake Pleasant/Scorpion Bay Marina (Miller brothers, July 12, 2020) — first confirmed Arizona ESD deaths
- **Lake Powell** electrocution (Alec McQueen, June 10, 2014) — straddles UT/AZ border, confirmed in ESDPA list
- Arizona now has at least 2 confirmed ESD incidents despite being listed as "0 known"
- ABC15 and azfamily.com (Fox10) have multiple YouTube videos with full transcripts on the Miller brothers case

### Pacific Northwest (WA/OR/ID — no new incidents found)
- Lucas Ritz (1999, Multnomah Channel/Willamette River, Oregon) is confirmed and well-documented — this is the ESDPA founder's case, already known
- No new Washington State or Oregon incidents found despite extensive searching
- No YouTube coverage of PNW-specific incidents beyond the Lucas Ritz educational videos
- The gap here may be genuine (fewer electrified private docks, different marina culture) or may require deeper local searches using KING5, KOMO, KGW archives

### Carolinas (NC/SC — no new incidents found)
- WCNC Charlotte produced a 2017 ESD awareness video (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MkY4TTAuIOU) citing Kayla Matos and Carmen Johnson — no North/South Carolina incidents specifically
- Lake Norman, Lake Murray, Lake Hartwell, Lake Keowee searches yielded no ESD incidents
- The Carolinas gap appears real for YouTube-accessible coverage — local searches of WCNC, WSOC, WLTX archives needed

### Upper Midwest (MN/WI — incidents found for MN)
- **Minnesota**: Two confirmed ESD deaths found — Robert Stoen (Pokegama Lake, 2012) and Daniel Peterson (Eagle Lake, 2013)
- WCCO CBS Minnesota has a 3:34 YouTube video (2016) on the Dan Peterson case with expert testimony
- **Wisconsin**: No ESD incidents found despite searching. Wisconsin lake culture mirrors Minnesota; gap likely reflects underreporting
- Minnesota ESD incidents cluster in northern and central MN lake regions (Cohasset/Pokegama Lake in Itasca County; Big Lake/Eagle Lake in Sherburne County)

### Northeast (NY/PA/NJ/MD)
- **New Jersey**: Kayla Matos (Toms River, 2017) — two CBS New York YouTube videos with full transcripts
- **Pennsylvania**: James DeAngelo (Monongahela River, 2021) — two WTAE Pittsburgh YouTube videos; inspired state ESD legislation
- **New York**: New Rochelle marina near-miss (2023) — web coverage only, no confirmed YouTube video
- **Maryland**: No incidents found

## Methodology Notes

- Brave Web Search `site:youtube.com` queries were effective for major incidents with dedicated videos
- Perplexity Search provided more detailed transcript content and corroborating articles
- The ESDPA incident list PDFs (both the Mike Holt version and the updated 8/15/25 version) were critical for verifying existing cataloguing
- Pool electrocutions (TX 2013, TX 2020) were found alongside dock/marina incidents — both involve the same ESD mechanism and belong in the database even if ESDPA traditionally focuses on marinas
- The James DeAngelo Foundation and PA Senate legislation confirm community-level recognition of a PA ESD death not formally attributed in the ESDPA list
