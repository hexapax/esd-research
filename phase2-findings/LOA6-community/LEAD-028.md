# LEAD-028: Jesse Hamric — Smith Mountain Lake, Virginia (July 4, 2024)

## Status
LEAD — NEEDS VERIFICATION (not in known deduplication list; confirmed fatal; stray voltage at dock confirmed by shock alert tester; investigation ongoing as of reporting)

## Basic Info
- **Date:** July 4, 2024 (early morning, ~4:00 a.m.)
- **Location:** Private dock on Lee Drive, Huddleston, Bedford County, Virginia (Smith Mountain Lake)
- **Victim:** Jesse Hamric, 18, of Steamboat Springs, Colorado (recent high school graduate; visiting Virginia with family; son of a school principal)
- **Outcome:** Fatal — electrocuted by stray voltage in lake water; two others who jumped in to help were also shocked (non-fatal injuries)

## Mechanism
Jesse Hamric dived into Smith Mountain Lake near a private dock at approximately 4 a.m. He immediately became distressed in the water. Two other people who saw him in distress jumped into the water to help and immediately felt electrical shocks. Bedford County Sheriff's Office determined the electrocution was related to stray voltage at a dock. The Smith Mountain Lake Marine Fire and Rescue Department's shock alert tester found voltage in the water. Rescue fireboats were dispatched. Hamric's body was later recovered from the lake.
