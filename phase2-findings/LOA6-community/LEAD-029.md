# LEAD-029: Joseph Salute & Maxwell Casillas — Lake Montclair, Virginia (June 2001)

## Status
LEAD — NEEDS VERIFICATION (may appear in ESDPA list as Virginia's only documented ESD prior to 2024; confirmed by Washington Post and police)

## Basic Info
- **Date:** June 22, 2001 (Saturday)
- **Location:** Lake Montclair, Prince William County, Virginia (Timber Ridge Drive / Dumfries area, Montclair community)
- **Victims:** 
  - Joseph John Salute, 15
  - Maxwell Scott Casillas, 11
  (Two other boys, including a 12-year-old, were also present and survived)
- **Outcome:** Fatal for both boys — electrocuted/drowned after being shocked by electrical current from a docked aluminum pontoon boat

## Mechanism
The two boys jumped into Lake Montclair for a swim. They were shocked by an electrical current coming from a docked aluminum pontoon boat. Police determined the boat had two battery chargers connected to a crudely modified extension cord from a house, without proper permits. The extension cord and outlet attached to the pontoon boat were "crudely rigged." Police were initially unsure whether the boys were directly electrocuted or drowned after being disoriented by the shock.
