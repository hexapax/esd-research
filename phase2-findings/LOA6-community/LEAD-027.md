# LEAD-027: Timothy Miller & Michael Miller — Lake Pleasant, Arizona (July 2020)

## Status
LEAD — NEEDS VERIFICATION (not in known deduplication list; MCSO confirmed ESD from modified boat electrical system; well-documented)

## Basic Info
- **Date:** July 12, 2020 (Sunday evening, ~6:30 p.m.)
- **Location:** Scorpion Bay Marina, Lake Pleasant, Peoria, Maricopa County, Arizona
- **Victims:** 
  - Timothy Miller, 53 (found below dock)
  - Michael Miller, 50 (taken to hospital, later pronounced dead)
- **Outcome:** Fatal for both brothers; girlfriend of one suffered burn marks on feet and legs (survived)

## Mechanism
The Miller brothers, their wives, and a girlfriend were on the family boat docked at Scorpion Bay Marina at Lake Pleasant. Timothy jumped into the water for an unknown reason and immediately experienced what witnesses described as electrical current. When Michael jumped in to save his brother, he also experienced the same symptoms. One wife also jumped in briefly. When she exited, the boat was unplugged. MCSO investigation determined the brothers had intentionally bypassed their boat's electrical safety features and improperly modified the boat's electrical connection system. The boat's system was not compatible with the marina's receptacle. The marina's own electrical infrastructure was found to be operating properly. Electricity from the modified boat system energized the water.
