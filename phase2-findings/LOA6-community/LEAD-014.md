## Mechanism
Forum poster on PontoonForums.com described two incidents: (1) The poster's father "dove into the water to take a swim, and felt the tingle. Unfortunately, the juice paralyzed his vocal cords. Somehow he found the strength to climb up the ladder." (2) A separate occasion when the boat was brought into the boathouse at night, "sparks flying when our pontoon boat touched the cables holding up the rack." The poster noted seeing many fish floating around their boathouse in the days prior. A faulty wire appeared to be the cause.
