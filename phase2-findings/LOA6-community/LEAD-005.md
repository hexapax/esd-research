# LEAD-005: James DeAngelo — Monongahela River / Beach Club Marina, New Eagle, Pennsylvania, July 4, 2021

## Status
VERIFIED (news reporting + ESDPA press release + coroner involvement confirmed)

## Basic Info
- **Date:** July 4, 2021
- **Location:** Beach Club Marina, New Eagle, Washington County, Pennsylvania (on the Monongahela River)
- **Victim:** James DeAngelo, 23, of Venetia, Pennsylvania
- **Outcome:** Fatal (pronounced dead at Monongahela Valley Hospital at 8:18 p.m.)

## Mechanism
DeAngelo was swimming near a boat docked at Beach Club Marina. He was observed to be unresponsive and pulled from the water. Eyewitness accounts indicated he may have experienced electric shock. Reports indicate three other swimmers in the same area also suffered shocks, which investigators stated made ESD a likely cause. The U.S. Coast Guard Auxiliary issued a public statement following the death alerting about ESD as a hidden danger at marinas.
