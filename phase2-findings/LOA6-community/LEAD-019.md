# LEAD-019: Marina Shores Prior Incident — Unidentified ESD Event at Same Marina, 4 Days Before Gabriel Gonzalez Death, July 6, 2025

## Status
LEAD — NEEDS VERIFICATION (referenced in wrongful death lawsuit; no separate news coverage found)

## Basic Info
- **Date:** July 6, 2025 (four days before the Gabriel Gonzalez death on July 10)
- **Location:** Marina Shores Marina, Burns Waterway, Portage, Porter County, Indiana (same location as LEAD-002)
- **Victim:** Unknown
- **Outcome:** Unknown (described as "a similar electrical shock event" in the wrongful death complaint)

## Mechanism
Per the Schafer & Schafer wrongful death complaint filed on behalf of the Gonzalez family: "This tragic incident was not isolated; four days earlier, a similar electrical shock event occurred at the same marina. The marina had knowledge of this prior event and yet failed to warn patrons or address the hazard, violating required safety codes." No further details about the July 6 incident are provided in the complaint.
