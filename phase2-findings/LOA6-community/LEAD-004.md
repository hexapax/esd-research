# LEAD-004: Unnamed Forsyth County Man Near-Miss — Lake Lanier, Georgia, circa 2013

## Status
LEAD — NEEDS VERIFICATION (single secondary source, name and date not confirmed)

## Basic Info
- **Date:** Approximate — circa 2013 (referenced in a 2016 WSBTV news report as "about three years ago")
- **Location:** Private dock, Lake Lanier, Forsyth County, Georgia
- **Victim:** Unnamed adult male (Forsyth County resident)
- **Outcome:** Non-fatal near-miss (shocked while swimming; managed to get himself and his daughter out of the water)

## Mechanism
A Forsyth County man was swimming off a family dock at Lake Lanier when he felt an electric shock. He was able to get himself and his daughter out of the water. The nature of the electrical fault was not described. The incident was mentioned in a 2016 WSBTV investigative report on ESD following Carmen Johnson's Alabama death.
