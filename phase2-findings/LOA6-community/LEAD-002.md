# LEAD-002: Gabriel Gonzalez — Marina Shores (Burns Waterway), Portage, Indiana, July 10, 2025

## Status
VERIFIED (Indiana DNR press release + law firm complaint + news coverage)

## Basic Info
- **Date:** July 10, 2025
- **Location:** Marina Shores Marina, "D dock," Burns Waterway (Lake Michigan harbor), Portage, Porter County, Indiana
- **Victim:** Gabriel Gonzalez, 21, of Hebron, Indiana
- **Outcome:** Fatal (cause of death: drowning secondary to electrocution, per Porter County Coroner)
- **Also injured:** Francis Gonzalez, 23 (brother), hospitalized and released

## Mechanism
An object belonging to a nearby boat owner fell into the water from D dock. Gabriel Gonzalez voluntarily entered the water to retrieve it for the owner. After retrieving the object, he swam toward D dock and entered a field of electricity, going under the surface. His brother Francis jumped in to rescue him, was affected by the electrified area but stayed on the surface, and was rescued by a Good Samaritan with a throwable flotation device. The electricity source was determined, with help from a licensed electrician, to be a piece of equipment belonging to a nearby slip holder. Crucially, a similar electrical shock event had occurred at the same marina four days earlier (July 6, 2025), and the marina allegedly failed to warn patrons or address the hazard.
