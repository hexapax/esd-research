# LEAD-041: Lake Hamilton, Arkansas — September 15, 2001 Farr Shores ESD Death

## Status
LEAD — NEEDS VERIFICATION (may already be in ESDPA list as known incident — check against FI-2007-003 cluster IDs)

## Basic Info
- **Date:** September 15, 2001
- **Location:** Farr Shores, Lake Hamilton, Hot Springs, Arkansas
- **Victim:** Unidentified adult male (attempted rescuer); unidentified girl (survived)
- **Outcome:** Fatal (rescuer) + Non-fatal injury (girl)

## Mechanism
A girl received an electric shock after water-sliding into the water at the stern of a boat. A man attempting to rescue her drowned. The cause was reverse polarity, neutral-to-ground fault in a light fixture on the boat, with poor bonding to the service. The boat was unplugged in time to save the girl but not the rescuer.
