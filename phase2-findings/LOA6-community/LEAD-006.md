# LEAD-006: Two Brothers (Ages 50 & 53) — Lake Pleasant / Scorpion Bay Marina, Peoria, Arizona, July 12, 2020

## Status
VERIFIED (Maricopa County Sheriff's Office confirmed ESD ruling; multiple news sources)

## Basic Info
- **Date:** July 12, 2020
- **Location:** Scorpion Bay Marina, Lake Pleasant, Peoria, Arizona (north of Phoenix)
- **Victim:** Two brothers, ages 53 and 50 (names not identified in initial searches)
- **Outcome:** Both fatal; a third person was injured (critical condition); two others present without life-threatening injuries

## Mechanism
The two brothers had "intentionally bypassed their boat's electrical safety features and improperly modified the electrical connection system," according to MCSO investigators. This modification prevented the boat from properly connecting with the marina's electrical system, causing the boat's underwater metals to become energized. The brothers and at least one other person entered the water near the boat at the end of a dock and were electrocuted. Investigators recreated the accident conditions and attributed the fatalities to electric shock drowning.
